# Rune of the Fort

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Virki** (Dwarven for 'Fortress/Stronghold')
- **Hlíf** (Dwarven for 'Defense')
> Meaning: "The Portable Stronghold"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Strengthens defenses or structures, making them harder to penetrate, and granting the wearer enhanced defensive stability.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+1 natural armor bonus** to AC.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Fortitude saves.
- **Location 3:** The wearer cannot be pushed or knocked prone while they are stationary.
- **Location 4:** Natural armor bonus increases to **+2**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Wall of Stone*.
- **Location 6:** Natural armor bonus increases to **+4**. The wearer gains **DR 5/Magic**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer and all adjacent allies gain the benefits of a *Globe of Invulnerability* once per day for 1 minute.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the armor of garrison commanders.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized fortress gate with three bars, glowing with a deep dull silver light.

---

## **The Legend of the Fort**
- Created for the **Defenders of the Gate**, who held a fortress against an entire dragon army.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Stronghold Thorin:** A smith who specialized in defensive architecture and translated it into metal.
