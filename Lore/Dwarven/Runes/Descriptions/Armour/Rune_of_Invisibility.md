# Rune of Invisibility

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ósýn** (Dwarven for 'Invisible/Unseen')
- **Hams** (Dwarven for 'Skin/Form')
> Meaning: "The Unseen Skin"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The wearer can become invisible for a limited time, allowing for stealthy movement or escape.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per long rest, the wearer can cast *Invisibility* for **10 minutes**.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** The invisibility is not broken by taking the *Help an Ally* action.
- **Location 4:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Greater Invisibility* for 1 round.
- **Location 5:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 6:** The wearer can use the invisibility as a swift action.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of *Invisibility* as long as they do not attack or cast a spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Standard for high-ranking spies and assassins.

---

## **Visual**
A shifting, semi-transparent rune that seems to disappear when viewed from certain angles.

---

## **The Legend of Invisibility**
- Discovered in the **Lower-Lower-Levels**, where the shadows themselves are said to be invisible.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Orik the Silent:** A smith who was so quiet that his own mother couldn't hear him entering a room.
