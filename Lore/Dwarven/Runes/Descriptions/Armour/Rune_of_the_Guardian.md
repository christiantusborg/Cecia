# Rune of the Guardian

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vörðr** (Dwarven for 'Guardian/Warden')
- **Hlíf** (Dwarven for 'Protection')
> Meaning: "The Warden's Shield"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor acts as a proactive shield, allowing the wearer to magically block attacks and protect allies.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer can cast *Shield* once per day as a swift action.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can use their reaction to grant an adjacent ally a **+2 bonus to AC** against one attack.
- **Location 4:** Usage of *Shield* increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *Bodyguard* feat for free while wearing the armor.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Shield of Faith* on themselves or an ally.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of the *Shield* spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is the hallmark of the Royal Guards of Lagon.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized shield with an eye in the center, glowing with a steady silver-white light.

---

## **The Legend of the Guardian**
- First forged for the **Shield-Maidens of Eisenwacht**, who were sworn to protect the King at any cost.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Warden Thalric:** A master-at-arms who turned to smithing to create the perfect equipment for his men.
