# Rune of the Juggernaut's March

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Risni** (Dwarven for 'Giant/Unstoppable')
- **Ganga** (Dwarven for 'March/Walk')
> Meaning: "The March of the Unstoppable"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The wearer's armor does not slow their speed, even if it is medium or heavy armor, allowing them to march with tireless strength.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer's base land speed is not reduced by wearing medium or heavy armor.
- **Location 2:** The wearer is immune to the *Fatigue* effect from running or marching.
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on Strength checks to break objects or doors.
- **Location 4:** The wearer can carry a **Heavy Load** without their speed being reduced.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *Power Attack* feat for free while wearing the armor.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can use *Freedom of Movement* for 1 minute.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can walk through 1 foot of solid material (stone, metal) per round as if it were air.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is highly favored by heavy infantry and siege specialists.

---

## **Visual**
A heavy, solid block with legs, glowing with a deep orange light.

---

## **The Legend of the Juggernaut's March**
- Created by the **Siege-Breakers of Eisenwacht**, who needed to march through arrow fire and rubble without slowing down.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Thorbjorn the Heavy:** A smith who was famously large and believed that armor should be an asset, not a burden.
