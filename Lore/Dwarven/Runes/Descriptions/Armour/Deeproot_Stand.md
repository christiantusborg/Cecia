# Deeproot Stand

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Djúp** (Dwarven for 'Deep')
- **Rót** (Dwarven for 'Root')
> Meaning: "The Deeply Rooted Stand"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor grants the wearer stability and connection to the ground, making them nearly impossible to move or knock down.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on checks to resist being tripped or bull rushed.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Balance checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can choose to automatically succeed on one trip or bull rush resistance check per day.
- **Location 4:** The wearer cannot be moved against their will as long as they are on a solid stone surface.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *Stability* trait (if they don't already have it; if they do, the bonus increases to +8).
- **Location 6:** The wearer can cast *Entangle* once per day, but only on creatures touching the same ground.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is immune to any effect that would forcibly move them from their current space.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune requires the wearer to be in contact with the ground.

---

## **Visual**
Several vertical lines plunging downwards from a horizontal bar, glowing with a deep forest-green light.

---

## **The Legend of the Deeproot Stand**
- Legend tells of **Dagna the Rooted**, who held a bridge against a dragon's wing-buffet by rooting herself to the stone.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Dagna the Rooted:** A smith who drew inspiration from the ancient fungi-trees that grow in the deepest caverns.
