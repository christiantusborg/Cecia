# Quake Step

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Skjálfti** (Dwarven for 'Quake/Tremor')
- **Spor** (Dwarven for 'Step/Footprint')
> Meaning: "The Tremor Step"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The wearer’s steps cause minor tremors in the ground, unbalancing nearby foes and making them easier to strike.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per round, as a free action, the wearer can cause a minor tremor. All enemies within 5 feet take a **-1 penalty** on Balance checks for 1 round.
- **Location 2:** Radius increases to **10 feet**.
- **Location 3:** Enemies within the radius must succeed on a **DC 14 Reflex save** or be *Shaken* by the vibration.
- **Location 4:** Penalty to Balance checks increases to **-4**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Earthquake* (as a 5-foot radius area) as a standard action.
- **Location 6:** Radius increases to **15 feet**. Save DC increases to **18**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] Every step deals **1d4 force damage** to all enemies within 5 feet.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This effect only works on solid ground.

A series of concentric circles radiating from a footprint-shaped rune.

## **The Legend of the Quake Step**
- Created by the **Titan-Stompers**, a specialized unit of dwarves trained to fight Large and Huge creatures.
