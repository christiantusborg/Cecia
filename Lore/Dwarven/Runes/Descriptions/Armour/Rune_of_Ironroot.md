# Rune of Ironroot

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Járn** (Dwarven for 'Iron')
- **Rót** (Dwarven for 'Root')
> Meaning: "The Iron Root"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporarily roots the bearer to the ground, increasing defense but preventing movement.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** As a move action, the wearer can root themselves. They gain a **+2 bonus to AC** but cannot move until the start of their next turn.
- **Location 2:** Bonus to AC increases to **+4**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains **DR 2/-** while rooted.
- **Location 4:** Once per day, the wearer can root themselves as a swift action.
- **Location 5:** Bonus to AC increases to **+6**. DR increases to **5/-**.
- **Location 6:** Rooting lasts for 1 minute or until ended by the wearer.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer and all adjacent allies gain the AC and DR bonuses while rooted.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is foundational for front-line defenders.

---

## **Visual**
A series of iron-grey roots extending from a central point, glowing with a dull metallic light.

---

## **The Legend of the Ironroot**
- Created for the **Sentinels of the Stone**, who were sworn to never retreat.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Master Bjorn:** A smith who was famously large and used his weight to his advantage.
