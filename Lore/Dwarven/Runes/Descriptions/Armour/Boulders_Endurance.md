# Boulder's Endurance

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Bjarg** (Dwarven for 'Boulder')
- **Thol** (Dwarven for 'Endurance')
> Meaning: "The Endurance of the Stone"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor imbues the wearer with the supernatural toughness of the mountains, granting additional vitality and stamina.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains **+1 temporary hit point** per level while the armor is worn.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** on Fortitude saves.
- **Location 3:** Temporary hit points increase to **+2 per level**.
- **Location 4:** Bonus to Fortitude saves increases to **+2**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer is immune to **Fatigue**.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains **Damage Reduction 2/-**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer gains the *Toughness* feat for free and **DR 5/-**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Temporary hit points are refreshed once per day after a long rest.

---

## **Visual**
A thick, rounded rune that looks like a smooth river stone, glowing with a steady earthy-brown light.

---

## **The Legend of the Boulder's Endurance**
- First etched into the armor of the **Long-Walkers**, who spent months traveling through the deepest tunnels without rest.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Bjorn the Stamina-King:** A dwarf who was famous for being able to out-last anyone in a drinking contest or a tunnel-digging competition.
