# Rune of the Yawning Abyss

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Gína** (Dwarven for 'Yawn/Gape')
- **Dalkr** (Dwarven for 'Abyss/Void')
> Meaning: "The Abyss that Gazes Back"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
When the wearer is hit, the armor can invoke a terrifying aura that causes enemies to become frightened.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, when hit by an attack, the wearer can force the attacker to succeed on a **DC 14 Will save** or become *Shaken* for 1d4 rounds.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** The save DC increases to **16**.
- **Location 4:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** On a failure, the attacker is instead *Frightened* for 1 round.
- **Location 6:** Every enemy within 10 feet must succeed on the save when the effect triggers.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The armor creates a permanent 10-foot aura of *Fear*.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Mind-affecting fear effect.

---

## **Visual**
A void-black rune that seems to suck in all light around it, resembling a bottomless pit.

---

## **The Legend of the Yawning Abyss**
- Discovered in the **Lower-Lower-Levels**, where the shadows themselves are said to have teeth.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Varkas the Grim:** A smith who studied the psychology of terror to create the ultimate defensive weapon.
