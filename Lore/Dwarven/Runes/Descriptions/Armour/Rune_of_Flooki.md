# Rune of Flooki

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Flóki** (Dwarven for 'Trickster/Illusionist')
> Meaning: "The Rune of the Trickster"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Offers the user a trick or illusion, allowing them to deceive their opponents or make a stealthy escape.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer can cast *Minor Image* once per day.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Bluff and Sleight of Hand checks.
- **Location 3:** Once per day, the wearer can become *Invisible* for 1 round as a reaction to being hit.
- **Location 4:** Usage of *Minor Image* increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer can cast *Mirror Image* once per day.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Major Image*.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can use *Hallucinatory Terrain* once per day.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This is an illusion effect.

---

## **Visual**
A smiling face with one eye closed, glowing with a mischievous green light.

---

## **The Legend of Flooki**
- Named after the legendary **Flooki the Fool**, who was said to be so tricky that he could steal the beard from a sleeping giant.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Flooki the Fool:** A renegade smith who loved to play pranks on his fellow craftsmen.
