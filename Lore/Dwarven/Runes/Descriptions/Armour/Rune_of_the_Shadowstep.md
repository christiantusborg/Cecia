# Rune of the Shadowstep

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Skuggi** (Dwarven for 'Shadow')
- **Stígr** (Dwarven for 'Step/Path')
> Meaning: "The Shadow-Step Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Once per long rest, the wearer can teleport between shadows, allowing for rapid movement and surprise attacks.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per long rest, the wearer can teleport up to **30 feet** as a move action (as per *Dimension Door*, but must start and end in shadow).
- **Location 2:** Range increases to **60 feet**.
- **Location 3:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 4:** After teleporting, the wearer is *Invisible* for 1 round.
- **Location 5:** Range increases to **120 feet**.
- **Location 6:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can use *Shadow Step* as a swift action.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Requires shadowy illumination or darkness at both the origin and the destination.

---

## **Visual**
A void-black rune that seems to trail wisps of shadow even in bright light.

---

## **The Legend of the Shadowstep**
- Forged for the **Silent Assassins of the Night**, who needed a way to penetrate the most secure fortresses.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Nyx the Dark:** A smith who lived in absolute darkness and claimed he could "walk through the gaps in the world."
