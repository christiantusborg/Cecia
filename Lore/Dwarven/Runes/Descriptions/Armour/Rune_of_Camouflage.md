# Rune of Camouflage

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Dylja** (Dwarven for 'Conceal/Hide')
- **Hams** (Dwarven for 'Skin/Form')
> Meaning: "The Concealing Skin"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor changes its appearance to match the surrounding natural environment, granting advantage on stealth checks.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Stealth checks in natural environments (forests, caves, etc.).
- **Location 2:** The wearer can use the *Hide in Plain Sight* ability while in natural environments.
- **Location 3:** Bonus increases to **+5**.
- **Location 4:** The wearer can cast *Pass Without Trace* at will.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can become *Invisible* for 10 minutes.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains a **+10 bonus** on Stealth checks while stationary in a natural environment.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of a *Nondetection* spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is favored by rangers and woodland scouts.

---

## **Visual**
A shifting, leaf-like pattern that changes color from green to brown to grey depending on the surroundings.

---

## **The Legend of the Camouflage**
- Forged by the **Wild-Walkers**, a clan of dwarves who chose to live in the surface forests rather than the deep mines.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Sylvan the Hidden:** A dwarf who was so in tune with nature that he could walk through a forest without snapping a single twig.
