# Forgeheart

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Afl** (Dwarven for 'Forge')
- **Hjarta** (Dwarven for 'Heart')
> Meaning: "The Heart of the Forge"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor is imbued with the tireless energy of the Great Forge, granting the wearer resistance to fatigue and exhaustion.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on saves against fatigue and exhaustion.
- **Location 2:** The wearer only needs 4 hours of sleep to gain the benefits of a long rest.
- **Location 3:** The wearer is immune to **Fatigue**.
- **Location 4:** If the wearer becomes exhausted, they are instead treated as fatigued.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on saves against poison and disease.
- **Location 6:** The wearer is immune to **Exhaustion**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer's base land speed increases by **10 feet** as the armor "pushes" them forward.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is highly valued by marathon runners and long-term expedition members.

---

## **Visual**
A glowing red heart-shape with a stylized anvil inside it.

---

## **The Legend of the Forgeheart**
- Legend says it was first created for the **Deep-Miners** who worked in shifts that lasted for weeks.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Kala the Tireless:** A female dwarf who was famous for her legendary work ethic and her ability to out-forge an entire guild.
