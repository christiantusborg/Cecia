# Rune of Blazing Retaliation

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sker** (Dwarven for 'Scar/Cut')
- **Logi** (Dwarven for 'Flame')
> Meaning: "The Flaming Retribution"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
When struck by a melee attack, the armor emits a burst of intense fire, punishing the attacker for their hubris.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** When hit by a melee attack, the attacker takes **1d4 fire damage**.
- **Location 2:** Damage increases to **1d6**.
- **Location 3:** Damage increases to **1d8**. The attacker must succeed on a **DC 14 Reflex save** or catch fire.
- **Location 4:** Damage increases to **2d6**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can activate a "Inferno Aura" for 1 minute, dealing **1d10 fire damage** to all adjacent enemies at the start of their turn.
- **Location 6:** Damage increases to **3d6**. Save DC increases to **18**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The fire is *Holy Fire*, dealing half damage as fire and half as divine energy (bypassing fire resistance).

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This effect can trigger multiple times per round.

---

## **Visual**
Jagged orange runes that flare up with a blinding light whenever the armor is struck.

---

## **The Legend of the Blazing Retaliation**
- Forged by **Vulkan the Vengeful**, who was tired of being surrounded by goblins and wanted a way to "fight back without moving."

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Vulkan the Vengeful:** A smith who was famously grumpy and believed that every strike against him should be repaid ten-fold.
