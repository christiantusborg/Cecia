# Rune of Anvilheart

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Steðja** (Dwarven for 'Anvil')
- **Hjarta** (Dwarven for 'Heart')
> Meaning: "The Heart of the Anvil"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Provides guidance and strength for crafting tasks, granting advantage on checks and increasing efficiency.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Weaponsmithing) and Craft (Armorsmithing) checks.
- **Location 2:** Crafting takes **25% less time**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can *Take 10* on Craft checks even in stressful situations.
- **Location 4:** Bonus to Craft checks increases to **+5**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can automatically succeed on a single Craft check (DC 20 or lower).
- **Location 6:** Crafting takes **50% less time**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is considered a "Master-Smith" and can craft artifacts given the right materials.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is highly valued by master-smiths.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized heart with a hammer inside it, glowing with a warm orange light.

---

## **The Legend of the Anvilheart**
- Legend says it was first created by a smith who loved his work so much that his own heart became an anvil.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **The First Smith:** A mythical figure who is said to have forged the world itself.
