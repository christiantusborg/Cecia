# Rune of Grundyr

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Grund** (Dwarven for 'Ground/Base')
- **Dyr** (Dwarven for 'Door/Guard')
> Meaning: "The Guardian of the Ground"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Increases stability, granting advantage on saving throws against being knocked prone or moved against the wearer's will.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on saves to avoid being knocked prone.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Balance checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can choose to automatically succeed on one save against being knocked prone per day.
- **Location 4:** Bonus to avoid being knocked prone increases to **+8**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *Stability* trait (if they don't already have it).
- **Location 6:** The wearer cannot be moved against their will by any effect of Medium size or smaller.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is immune to any effect that would forcibly move them from their current space.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is standard for heavy infantry.

---

## **Visual**
A solid, horizontal bar with three vertical bars extending downwards, glowing with a deep earthy-brown light.

---

## **The Legend of Grundyr**
- Created for the **Stone-Sentinels**, who were sworn to hold their position no matter what.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Bjorn the Stout:** A smith who was famously heavy-footed and used his weight to his advantage.
