# Rune of Lightness

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Léttir** (Dwarven for 'Light/Easy')
- **Vætt** (Dwarven for 'Weight')
> Meaning: "The Weight-Reducing Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Reduces the effective weight of the armor, allowing the wearer to move faster and with less effort.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Reduces the armor's weight by **50%**.
- **Location 2:** Increases the wearer's base land speed by **5 feet**.
- **Location 3:** Reduces the armor's weight by **75%**.
- **Location 4:** The armor check penalty is reduced by **2**.
- **Location 5:** Increases the wearer's base land speed by **10 feet**.
- **Location 6:** The armor is treated as one category lighter for all purposes.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The armor has **no weight** and grants the wearer a fly speed of 20 feet (clumsy).

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is standard for long-range scouts and mountaineers.

---

## **Visual**
A feather-shaped rune glowing with a soft, pale-blue light.

---

## **The Legend of the Lightness**
- Created by **Haldor the Swift**, who wanted to climb the highest peaks without being slowed by his heavy armor.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Haldor the Swift:** A mountain-climbing specialist who believed that speed was the best defense.
