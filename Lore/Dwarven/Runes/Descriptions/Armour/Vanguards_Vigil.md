# Vanguard’s Vigil

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Varðar** (Dwarven for 'Vanguard/Forward-guard')
- **Vaka** (Dwarven for 'Vigil/Watch')
> Meaning: "The Vanguard's Eye"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Armor provides a bonus to Initiative and alertness, ensuring the wearer is always ready for battle.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Initiative checks.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Spot and Listen checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer cannot be caught flat-footed at the start of combat.
- **Location 4:** Bonus to Initiative increases to **+4**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *Uncanny Dodge* ability while wearing the armor.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can use *See Invisibility* for 10 minutes.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer and all allies within 30 feet gain a **+2 bonus** on Initiative.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Popular among scouts and first-strike units.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized eye looking forward over a mountain range, glowing with a sharp white light.

---

## **The Legend of the Vanguard's Vigil**
- Created for the **First-In Squads** who were responsible for opening the doors to enemy fortresses.
