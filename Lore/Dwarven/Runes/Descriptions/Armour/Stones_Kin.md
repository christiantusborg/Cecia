# Stone's Kin

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Steinn** (Dwarven for 'Stone')
- **Kyn** (Dwarven for 'Kin/Relative')
> Meaning: "The Kin of the Stone"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor magically shifts its color and texture to blend in with rocky and subterranean environments, granting the wearer enhanced stealth.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Hide and Move Silently checks in rocky or underground terrain.
- **Location 2:** Bonus increases to **+4**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can use the *Hide in Plain Sight* ability while in subterranean environments.
- **Location 4:** Bonus increases to **+6**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can become *Invisible* (as per the spell) for 1 minute while in a rocky area.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains a **+10 bonus** on Hide checks if they remain stationary against a stone wall.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can phase through up to 5 feet of solid stone once per day.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is essential for scouts and ambushers.

---

## **Visual**
A mottled, grey-and-brown rune that seems to disappear when viewed from certain angles.

---

## **The Legend of the Stone's Kin**
- Developed by the **Shadow-Scouts** of the Protectorate to allow them to monitor enemy movements without being detected.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Orik the Silent:** A smith who was said to be so quiet that his own mother couldn't hear him entering a room.
