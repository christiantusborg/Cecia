# Rune of the Battlecaster

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Orrusta** (Dwarven for 'Battle')
- **Galdr** (Dwarven for 'Spell/Magic')
> Meaning: "The Battle-Mage's Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Allows the wearer to ignore arcane spell failure from armor for low-level spells, with the effectiveness scaling as more runes are added.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Reduces Arcane Spell Failure by **5%** and allows the wearer to ignore ASF for 0-level spells.
- **Location 2:** Reduces ASF by **10%** and allows ignoring ASF for 1st-level spells.
- **Location 3:** Reduces ASF by **15%**.
- **Location 4:** Allows ignoring ASF for 2nd-level spells.
- **Location 5:** Reduces ASF by **20%**.
- **Location 6:** Allows ignoring ASF for 3rd-level spells.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] Reduces ASF by **30%** and allows ignoring ASF for all spells up to 4th level.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is essential for armored casters and paladins who use arcane magic.

---

## **Visual**
A rune that looks like a hand clutching a staff and a sword simultaneously, glowing with a deep violet light.

---

## **The Legend of the Battlecaster**
- Forged by the **Mage-Smiths of Lagon** to allow their sorcerers to fight in the front lines with their soldiers.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Magister Kael:** A wizard who turned to smithing to create the perfect equipment for his "Spell-Swords."
