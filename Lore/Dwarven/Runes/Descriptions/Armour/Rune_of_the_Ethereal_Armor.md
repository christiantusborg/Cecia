# Rune of the Ethereal Armor

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Andi** (Dwarven for 'Spirit')
- **Hlíf** (Dwarven for 'Armor')
> Meaning: "The Armor of the Spirits"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Once per long rest, the wearer can cause their armor to grant them the effects of the "Blink" spell, making them harder to hit.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per long rest, the wearer can activate *Blink* for 1 minute.
- **Location 2:** The armor gains a **+1 deflection bonus** to AC while blinking.
- **Location 3:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 4:** The miss chance for attackers increases to **60%**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer can cast *Etherealness* once per week.
- **Location 6:** Usage increases to **3/day**. Deflection bonus increases to **+3**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The armor is permanently "ghost-touched" and grants a +2 deflection bonus at all times.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This is a transmutation effect.

---

## **Visual**
A pale, translucent rune that emits a faint, ghostly hum.

---

## **The Legend of the Ethereal Armor**
- Forged by **Odin the Wise** for his personal guard to protect them from both physical and magical threats.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Odin the Wise:** A legendary smith-king who understood the balance between the material and the spiritual.
