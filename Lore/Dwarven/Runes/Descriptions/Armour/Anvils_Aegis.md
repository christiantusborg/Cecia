# Anvil's Aegis

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Steðja** (Dwarven for 'Anvil')
- **Hlíf** (Dwarven for 'Protection/Shield')
> Meaning: "The Anvil's Shield"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor becomes as unyielding as a master-forged anvil, granting the wearer enhanced protection against critical hits and precise strikes.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** to AC against critical confirmation rolls.
- **Location 2:** The wearer is immune to the *Sneak Attack* of creatures with 3 or fewer HD.
- **Location 3:** Bonus to AC against critical hits increases to **+2**.
- **Location 4:** The armor has a **25% chance** to negate a critical hit or sneak attack (as per the *Fortification* property).
- **Location 5:** Bonus to AC against critical hits increases to **+4**.
- **Location 6:** Negation chance increases to **50%**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] Negation chance increases to **75%**. The armor is treated as *Heavy Fortification*.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the armor of front-line defenders and shield-bearers.

---

## **Visual**
A solid, square rune that glows with a deep, dull iron-grey light.

---

## **The Legend of the Anvil's Aegis**
- Created by **Thorin the Unyielding**, who once stood in a tunnel for three days, blocking an orcish horde with his own body and armor.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Thorin the Unyielding:** A legendary smith-warrior who believed that the best offense was a defense that could not be broken.
