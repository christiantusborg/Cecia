# Rune of the Serpent's Coils

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ormr** (Dwarven for 'Serpent/Snake')
- **Vafinn** (Dwarven for 'Wrapped/Coiled')
> Meaning: "The Serpent's Grip"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor becomes incredibly slippery and flexible, granting the wearer advantage on escaping grapples and restraints.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on checks to escape a grapple or avoid being grappled.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Escape Artist checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can move through spaces occupied by enemies without provoking attacks of opportunity.
- **Location 4:** The armor is treated as being under the effect of *Freedom of Movement* for 1 round once per day.
- **Location 5:** Bonus to escape grapples increases to **+8**.
- **Location 6:** The wearer can squeeze through spaces as if they were one size category smaller.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of the *Freedom of Movement* spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is popular among monks and those who fight large, tentacled creatures.

---

## **Visual**
A series of interlocking, snake-like patterns that seem to slide over each other.

---

## **The Legend of the Serpent's Coils**
- Created by **Sigurd the Slithery**, who was once swallowed by a giant worm and survived by slipping out of its throat.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Sigurd the Slithery:** A smith who was famously thin and double-jointed, allowing him to work in the narrowest forge-spaces.
