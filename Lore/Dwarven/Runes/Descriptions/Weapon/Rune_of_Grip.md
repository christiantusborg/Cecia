# Rune of Grip

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Taka** (Dwarven for 'Take/Grip')
- **Fastr** (Dwarven for 'Fast/Firm')
> Meaning: "The Unfailing Grip"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune ensures that the weapon remains firmly in the wielder's hand, making it nearly impossible to disarm.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wielder gains a **+4 bonus** on checks to resist being disarmed.
- **Location 2:** The weapon can be retrieved as a **swift action** if it is within 30 feet (as per the *Returning* property).
- **Location 3:** The wielder is immune to being disarmed by non-magical means.
- **Location 4:** The weapon "sticks" to the wielder's hand; even if the wielder is unconscious, they do not drop the weapon.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wielder can automatically succeed on a disarm resistance check.
- **Location 6:** The weapon can be summoned to the wielder's hand from any distance on the same plane as a standard action.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The weapon and wielder are spiritually linked; the wielder can never be separated from the weapon unless they choose to be.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the weapons of clumsy warriors or those who fear losing their precious artifacts.

---

## **Visual**
A hand tightly grasping a vertical bar, etched in textured iron that feels slightly "sticky" to the touch.

---

## **The Legend of the Grip**
- Created by **Hodor the Butter-Fingered**, a smith who was tired of his apprentices dropping their tools in the forge.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Hodor the Butter-Fingered:** A clumsy but brilliant smith who specialized in ergonomic and secure weapon designs.
