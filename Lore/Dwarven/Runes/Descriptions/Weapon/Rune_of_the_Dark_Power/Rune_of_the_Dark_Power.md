# Rune of the Dark Power

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Myrkr** (Dwarven for 'Dark/Darkness')
- **Máttr** (Dwarven for 'Power/Might')
> Meaning: "The Might of the Void"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune allows the wielder to sacrifice their own life-force to unleash a devastating burst of strength. The wielder chooses how much power to draw from the void, with the risk of losing control growing alongside the intensity of the strike.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Medium)

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
*Note: This rune is activated as a free action upon a successful melee strike. The wielder chooses to roll any number of **d6** dice, from **1** up to the **Maximum Dice** listed for the current slot. Both the wielder and the target take the rolled damage (un-multiplied). The target then takes additional damage based on the slot's multiplier.*

- **Location 1:** **Max 2 Dice.** Target takes **Total Damage * 1.25**.
- **Location 2:** **Max 3 Dice.** Target takes **Total Damage * 1.25**.
- **Location 3:** **Max 4 Dice.** Target takes **Total Damage * 1.25**.
- **Location 4:** **Max 5 Dice.** Target takes **Total Damage * 1.5**.
- **Location 5:** **Max 6 Dice.** Target takes **Total Damage * 1.5**.
- **Location 6:** **Max 7 Dice.** Target takes **Total Damage * 1.5**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] **Max 8 Dice.** Target takes **Total Damage * 2**.

---

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules: The Risk of Loss**
Every time the rune is used, the wielder must make a **Will Save (DC 10 + number of dice rolled + rune slot level)**. 
- **Failure:** The wielder enters a **Berserk Frenzy** for 1d4 rounds, as they are overcome by the dark power. While in this state, they must attack the nearest creature (friend or foe) and cannot use the rune again until the frenzy ends.
- **Success:** The wielder retains control, but the strain leaves them **Fatigued** for 1 minute.

#### **4.4.1 Roleplay Rules (Optional)**
- **The Shadow's Touch:** Wielders of this rune often notice their shadows acting independently or growing longer and darker than they should be.

#### **4.4.2 Critical Hit Effects (Optional)**
- **Dark Resonance:** On a critical hit, the wielder takes no damage from the rune's use, while the target still takes the full (and multiplied) damage.

---

## **Visual**
The rune is a series of interlocking, irregular obsidian-like shards etched into the blade. When active, it bleeds a thick, oily black smoke that clings to the wielder's arm. Upon impact, a shockwave of shadow erupts from the point of contact.

---

## **The Legend of the Dark Power**
- First struck by **Thrain the Hollow**, a smith who survived the collapse of the Deep-Vein Mines by striking a bargain with something that lived in the dark spaces between the stones. He emerged with the knowledge of the *Myrkr-Máttr*, but he never spoke again, and his eyes remained as black as the void he'd encountered.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Thrain the Hollow:** A reclusive and unsettling dwarf who preferred to work in total darkness. He was said to have "forgotten his own name" and was eventually exiled from the Protectorate for "crafting things that should not be."
