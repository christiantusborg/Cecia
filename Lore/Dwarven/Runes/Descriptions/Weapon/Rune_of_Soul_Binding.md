# Rune of Soul Binding

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sál** (Dwarven for 'Soul')
- **Binda** (Dwarven for 'Bind/Tie')
> Meaning: "The Soul-Binder"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune is designed to house a soul within a weapon crystal, allowing the weapon to gain sentience or draw power from the essence of the departed.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+25**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The weapon can hold a soul of CR 5 or lower. The soul provides a +1 enhancement bonus to the weapon.
- **Location 2:** Enhancement bonus increases to **+2**.
- **Location 3:** The soul can grant the weapon one 1st-level spell effect (e.g., *Bless*) usable 1/day.
- **Location 4:** The weapon can hold a soul of CR 10 or lower. Enhancement bonus increases to **+3**.
- **Location 5:** The soul can grant the weapon one 3rd-level spell effect (e.g., *Haste*) usable 1/day.
- **Location 6:** Enhancement bonus increases to **+4**. The weapon becomes semi-sentient and can communicate telepathically with the wielder.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The weapon can hold a soul of any CR. It gains full sentience, its own personality, and a +5 enhancement bonus.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Requires a Masterwork Weapon Crystal to function.
- The soul's alignment may affect the weapon's behavior.

---

## **Visual**
A swirling, ghostly blue pattern that seems to move on its own. When a soul is present, the crystal glows with a soft, steady light.

---

## **The Legend of the Soul Binding**
- First developed by **Thrain the Necro-Smith**, who sought a way to preserve the wisdom of fallen kings within their own blades.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Thrain the Necro-Smith:** A controversial figure who believed that death was merely a transition to a more "durable" form.
