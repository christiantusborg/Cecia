# Cecia Sword Rune Creation System (v2.2)

This document serves as both the **Structured Design Prompt** and the **Markdown Template** for creating new Dwarven Sword Runes for the Cecia setting (D&D 3.5e).

---

## **Part 1: The Design Prompt**
*Use these steps to design a new rune. Provide these details when asking the assistant to generate a new rune.*

### **Step 1: Magical Concept**
Pick or describe the core theme (e.g., Fire, Ice, Shadow, Wind, Time, Life Drain, etc.).

### **Step 2: Slot Mechanics (1–7)**
Swords have 7 rune slots. Decide how the rune behaves:
- **Scaling:** Power grows with higher slot level (1 to 7).
- **Fixed Slot:** Works only in a specific slot (e.g., Slot 3).
- **Threshold:** Activates only if in Slot X or higher.

### **Step 3: Category**
- ⚔ **Combat:** Extra damage, debuffs, crowd-control.
- 🛡 **Enhancement:** Defense, parry, AC bonus.
- ☠️ **Curse:** Harmful/corrupting effects.
- 🎭 **Utility:** Movement, illusions, etc.

### **Step 4: Power Level (3.5e Benchmarks)**
- **Minor:** 1d4 damage or cantrip-level buff.
- **Lesser:** 1d6–1d8 damage or 1st-level spell effect.
- **Moderate:** 2nd–3rd level spell effects (*Flaming*, *Shocking*).
- **Strong:** 4th–5th level spell effects.
- **Legendary:** 6th+ level spell effects.
