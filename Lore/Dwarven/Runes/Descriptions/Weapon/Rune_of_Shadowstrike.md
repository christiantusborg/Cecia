# Rune of Shadowstrike

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Myrkr** (Dwarven for 'Dark/Shadow')
- **Slagr** (Dwarven for 'Strike')
> Meaning: "The Strike from Shadows"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The weapon becomes partially incorporeal, allowing it to bypass physical armor and strike directly at the target's essence.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, you can make an attack as a **touch attack**, ignoring armor and shield bonuses to AC.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** The weapon ignores **2 points of Damage Reduction** (except DR/Epic).
- **Location 4:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** Attacks ignore **5 points of DR**.
- **Location 6:** Every attack made while the wielder is in shadowy illumination or darkness is treated as a touch attack.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The blade is permanently "shadowed," dealing an additional **2d6 cold damage** on every hit and ignoring all non-magical armor.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Does not affect creatures with the [Ghost] or [Incorporeal] subtypes unless it also has the *Ghost Touch* property.

---

## **Visual**
The blade's edge seems to blur and trail shadows as it moves. The rune itself is a void-black mark that absorbs all light.

---

## **The Legend of the Shadowstrike**
- Forged in the **Deepest Dark** where light has never touched the stone, using shadow-essences gathered from the Plane of Shadow.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Erebus the Unseen:** A reclusive smith who claimed he could hear the whispers of the shadows and taught them how to bite.
