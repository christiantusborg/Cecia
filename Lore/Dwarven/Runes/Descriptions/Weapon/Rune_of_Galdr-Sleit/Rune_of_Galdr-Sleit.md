# Rune of Galdr-Sleit (Arcane Feedback)

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Galdr** (Dwarven for 'Magic' or 'Spell')
- **Sleit** (Dwarven for 'Sever' or 'Break')
> Meaning: "The Severing of Magic"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune creates a disruptive resonance when it strikes a creature capable of casting spells. It interferes with the flow of arcane or divine energy, starting as a mere distraction and scaling into a total suppression of the target's magical abilities.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
*Note: Save DCs and Concentration DCs for this rune are calculated as **10 + 1/2 Wielder's Level + Strength Modifier** (unless otherwise noted).*

- **Location 1:** **Arcane Distraction.** On hit, the target must make a **Concentration check (DC 15 + Spell Level)** or suffer a **-2 penalty to the Caster Level and DC** of the next spell they attempt to cast. They do not lose the spell if they fail.
- **Location 2:** **Resonant Interference.** As Location 1, but the penalty increases to **-4**.
- **Location 3:** **Momentary Silence.** On hit, the target must succeed on a **Will Save** or be unable to cast spells with verbal components for **1 round**.
- **Location 4:** **Mana-Lock.** On hit, the target must succeed on a **Will Save** or be unable to cast any spells for **3 rounds**. A target can spend a standard action to attempt a **Concentration check (DC 20 + highest spell level available)** to break the lock early.
- **Location 5:** **Deep Lock.** As Location 4, but the duration increases to **1 minute**, and the DC to break early increases by **+5**.
- **Location 6:** **Spell-Drain.** On hit, the target must succeed on a **Fortitude Save** or lose their highest-level prepared spell or spell slot currently available.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] **Arcane Nullification.** On hit, the target must succeed on a **Will Save** or have their connection to the weave severed. They are incapable of casting any spells or using spell-like abilities for **1 hour** (the equivalent of a short rest). Only *Greater Restoration* or *Heal* can end this effect early.

---

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- **Disruption:** If the wielder uses a Readied Action to strike a caster while they are casting, the DC of the target's Concentration check to avoid losing the spell increases by the level of this rune slot.

#### **4.4.1 Roleplay Rules (Optional)**
- **Mage-Hater's Mark:** Inscribing this rune is often seen as an act of open hostility toward magical colleges. Elven casters, in particular, find the presence of this rune deeply offensive.

#### **4.4.2 Critical Hit Effects (Optional)**
- **Arcane Burn:** On a critical hit, the target takes **1d6 points of damage per level of the highest spell they currently have prepared** as their own magic backfires into their body.

---

## **Visual**
The rune consists of three parallel vertical lines with a single horizontal line "cutting" through them at a sharp angle. It glows with a jittery, static-filled blue light. When it strikes a caster, sparks of blue electricity jump from the blade to the target's hands.

---

## **The Legend of the Galdr-Sleit**
- Forged by **Haldur Mage-Bane**, a dwarf who lost his clan to a single fireball from a rogue sorcerer. He dedicated his life to creating a weapon that would ensure no wizard would ever finish a chant in his presence again.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Haldur Mage-Bane:** A grim, silent dwarf who wore heavy iron plates even in the heat of the forge. He never used magic himself and believed that the world would be a better place if every wand was snapped and every scroll was burned.
