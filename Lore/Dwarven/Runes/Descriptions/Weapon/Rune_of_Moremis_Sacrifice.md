# Rune of Moremi's Sacrifice

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Moremi** (Foreign name of a legendary queen)
- **Fórn** (Dwarven for 'Sacrifice')
> Meaning: "The Sacrifice of Moremi"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune activates when an ally falls in battle, empowering the wielder with the strength of the fallen's legacy.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** If an ally within 30 feet is reduced to 0 HP, the wielder gains a **+1 morale bonus** to attack and damage rolls for 3 rounds.
- **Location 2:** Bonus increases to **+2**.
- **Location 3:** The wielder also gains **1d10 temporary hit points**.
- **Location 4:** Bonus increases to **+3**. Duration increases to 1 minute.
- **Location 5:** The wielder can cast *Cure Moderate Wounds* once per day, but only on the fallen ally.
- **Location 6:** The morale bonus increases to **+5**. The wielder becomes immune to fear for the duration.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] If an ally dies, the wielder can choose to take a -2 penalty to all stats for 24 hours to instantly cast *Breath of Life* (if available) or a similar effect on the deceased.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the weapons of guardians and holy warriors.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized heart wrapped in thorns, glowing with a warm, golden-red light.

---

## **The Legend of Moremi's Sacrifice**
- Named after a legendary queen from a distant land who sacrificed her most precious joy to save her people from invaders.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Adunni the Wise:** A foreign smith who traveled to the dwarven forges to share her culture's secrets of sacrificial magic.
