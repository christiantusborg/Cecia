# Rune of Harmonizing

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Samhljóð** (Dwarven for 'Harmony')
- **Rún** (Dwarven for 'Rune')
> Meaning: "The Rune of Attunement"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The blade attunes with the wielder’s magic, increasing the effectiveness and ease of their spellcasting while the weapon is held.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wielder gains a **+1 bonus** on Concentration checks.
- **Location 2:** The weapon can be used as a divine or arcane focus for spellcasting.
- **Location 3:** Bonus to Concentration increases to **+4**.
- **Location 4:** Once per day, the wielder can cast a spell with a casting time of 1 standard action as a **swift action** (as per Quicken Spell).
- **Location 5:** Increases the DC of any spell cast by the wielder by **+1**.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wielder can regain a spent 1st or 2nd level spell slot by "siphoning" energy from the blade.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The wielder ignores Arcane Spell Failure from light and medium armor while holding the blade.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is highly prized by Gish (fighter/mage) characters.

---

## **Visual**
A perfectly symmetrical rune that glows with a soft, shifting violet light.

---

## **The Legend of the Harmonizing Rune**
- Created by the **Wizards of the Mystic Voyagers** in collaboration with dwarven masters to bridge the gap between steel and spell.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **M'shen (Collaborator):** A Wizard of the Mystic Voyagers who understood that a warrior's weapon should be an extension of their soul, not just a tool.
