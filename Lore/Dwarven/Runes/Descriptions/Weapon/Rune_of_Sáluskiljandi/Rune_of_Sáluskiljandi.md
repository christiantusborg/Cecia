# Rune of Sáluskiljandi (Soul Splitter) - V4 (The Synchronized Strike)

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sálu** (Dwarven for 'Soul')
- **Skiljandi** (Dwarven for 'Splitter/Divider')
> Meaning: "The Divider of Souls"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The blade inflicts a temporary spiritual fracture. A single strike determines both the target's Wisdom penalty and the duration of their terror; after a few heartbeats, the soul mends and the effects vanish.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
*Note: All effects require a **Will save (DC 10 + 1/2 Wielder's Level + Charisma Modifier)**. On a failed save, roll the indicated die once. The result determines both the **Wisdom Penalty** and the **Duration** in rounds for both the penalty and the fear condition.*

- **Location 1:** Roll **1d2**. Target takes a Wisdom penalty equal to the result and is **Shaken** for that many rounds.
- **Location 2:** Roll **1d3**. Target takes a Wisdom penalty equal to the result and is **Shaken** for that many rounds.
- **Location 3:** Roll **1d4**. Target takes a Wisdom penalty equal to the result and is **Frightened** for that many rounds.
- **Location 4:** Roll **1d6**. Target takes a Wisdom penalty equal to the result and is **Frightened** for that many rounds.
- **Location 5:** Roll **1d8**. Target takes a Wisdom penalty equal to the result and is **Panicked (Fear)** for that many rounds.
- **Location 6:** Roll **1d10**. Target takes a Wisdom penalty equal to the result and is **Panicked (Fear)** for that many rounds.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] Roll **1d12**. Target takes a Wisdom penalty equal to the result and is **Panicked (Fear)** for that many rounds. If the die results in a 12, the target is also **Stunned** for 1 round.

---

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- **Mind-Affecting:** This is a mind-affecting fear effect.
- **Temporary Penalty:** Unlike standard ability damage, this is a **penalty** that lasts only for the duration specified by the die roll. It does not stack with itself from the same weapon; only the most severe penalty/duration applies.
- **Synchronized Recovery:** At the end of the duration, both the fear condition and the Wisdom penalty end simultaneously.

#### **4.4.1 Roleplay Rules (Optional)**
- **The Echo of Void:** For the duration of the effect, the victim’s eyes turn a dull, light-less violet.

#### **4.4.2 Critical Hit Effects (Optional)**
- **Deep Fracture:** On a critical hit, the duration/penalty result is **maximized** for the current rune slot (e.g., a 6 on a 1d6).

---

## **Visual**
A jagged violet line that pulses once when it strikes. A single spark of violet light jumps from the blade to the victim, fading slowly as the effect wears off.

---

## **The Legend of the Sáluskiljandi**
- Forged by **Thrain the Hollow**, who sought a way to incapacitate foes without leaving them permanently broken—allowing them to live with the memory of their own shattered will.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Thrain the Hollow:** A reclusive smith whose own Wisdom was said to have been drained by the very first prototype he forged. He finished the set in a trance, leaving behind runes that are as mathematically precise as they are cruel.
