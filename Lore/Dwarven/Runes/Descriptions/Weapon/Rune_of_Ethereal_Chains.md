# Rune of Ethereal Chains

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Andi** (Dwarven for 'Spirit/Ghost')
- **Keðja** (Dwarven for 'Chain')
> Meaning: "The Spirit Binder"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The sword can bind ethereal or spectral creatures, preventing them from phasing out or escaping to other planes.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The weapon gains the *Ghost Touch* property, allowing it to strike incorporeal creatures normally.
- **Location 2:** On a hit, an incorporeal creature must succeed on a **DC 15 Will save** or be unable to use its *Incorporeal Movement* or *Blink* abilities for 1 round.
- **Location 3:** Save DC increases to **18**.
- **Location 4:** The weapon can "hook" an ethereal creature, pulling it fully into the Material Plane for 1d4 rounds on a critical hit.
- **Location 5:** The wielder can use the weapon to cast *Dimensional Anchor* once per day on a target they hit.
- **Location 6:** The weapon can strike creatures in the Ethereal Plane even if the wielder is on the Material Plane.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The blade can trap a ghost or wraith within itself (as per *Trap the Soul*) if it delivers the killing blow.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is essential for hunters of the undead and extraplanar entities.

---

## **Visual**
The rune looks like two intertwining chains that seem to fade in and out of existence.

---

## **The Legend of the Ethereal Chains**
- Forged by **Beli the Exorcist** to deal with the Haunt of the Shadow Mines that claimed his entire clan.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Beli the Exorcist:** A grim, quiet dwarf who specialized in "quieting" restless spirits with his rune-etched maul.
