# Rune of Shiva's Dance

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Shiva** (Foreign name of a deity of destruction)
- **Dans** (Dwarven for 'Dance')
> Meaning: "The Dance of Destruction"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune allows the wielder to perform a whirlwind-like attack, striking all nearby enemies in a single, fluid motion.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wielder gains the *Whirlwind Attack* feat, even if they don't meet the prerequisites, but it can only be used once per day.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** When using Whirlwind Attack, the wielder deals an additional **1d6 fire damage** to all targets.
- **Location 4:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The wielder can use a move action to "Spin," granting them a **+4 bonus to AC** against ranged attacks for 1 round.
- **Location 6:** Whirlwind Attack can be used as a standard action instead of a full-round action.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The dance creates a 10-foot radius *Fire Storm* for 1 round.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune requires great agility and is often favored by dervishes and monks.

---

## **Visual**
Four arms holding different weapons, arranged in a circle. The rune glows with a flickering, blue flame.

---

## **The Legend of Shiva's Dance**
- Inspired by tales of a god of destruction from the far south, whose dance could end the world or create it anew.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Rishi the Forger:** A mystic who sought to capture the destructive beauty of the cosmic dance in steel.
