# Rune of the Gladiator

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Kempu** (Dwarven for 'Gladiator' or 'Champion')
- **Rún** (Dwarven for 'Rune' or 'Mark')
> Meaning: "The Mark of the Champion"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune magically guides the wielder's hands, granting them immediate proficiency with the weapon it is inscribed upon.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+0** (Easy)

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wielder is treated as having the appropriate **Weapon Proficiency** feat (Simple, Martial, or Exotic) for this specific weapon while wielding it.
- **Location 2:** As Location 1.
- **Location 3:** As Location 1.
- **Location 4:** As Location 1.
- **Location 5:** As Location 1.
- **Location 6:** As Location 1.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] **Cannot be placed here.** The spiritual complexity of a Soul Sword rejects such a basic martial imprint.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- **Non-Transferable:** This proficiency only applies to the specific weapon bearing the rune. It does not grant the wielder proficiency with other weapons of the same type.
- **Feat Prerequisites:** This rune does not allow the wielder to meet prerequisites for other feats (like *Weapon Focus* or *Power Attack*) unless they actually possess the proficiency feat naturally.

#### **4.4.1 Roleplay Rules (Optional)**
- **Arena Standard:** This rune is widely used in gladiatorial pits and by noble houses to quickly arm untrained levies in times of crisis.

#### **4.4.2 Critical Hit Effects (Optional)**
- **Instinctive Strike:** None. This rune is designed for basic competence, not combat flourishing.

---

## **Visual**
The rune appears as a bold, solid engraving of a stylized fist clutching a short vertical line. It glows with a warm, steady amber light that pulses faintly when the wielder's grip tightens.

---

## **The Legend of the Gladiator**
- First struck by **Brokk the Arena-Master**, a retired pit-fighter turned smith. Brokk realized that many talented fighters died simply because they couldn't master the exotic blades of the arena fast enough. He forged the *Kempurún* to level the playing field, ensuring that victory went to the bravest heart, not just the best-trained hand.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Brokk the Arena-Master:** A massive dwarf with a missing ear and more scars than skin. He was known for his Gruff exterior but had a secret soft spot for "unlikely heroes." He believed that every dwarf—no matter their station—should have the right to defend themselves with the best steel available.
