# Rune of Keen Edge

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Skar** (Dwarven for 'Sharp')
- **Egg** (Dwarven for 'Edge')
> Meaning: "The Keen Edge"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
A fundamental rune that hones the weapon's edge to a supernatural sharpness, increasing its critical threat range.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Increases the critical threat range by **+1** (e.g., 20 becomes 19-20).
- **Location 2:** **N/A**
- **Location 3:** Increases the critical threat range by **+2**.
- **Location 4:** **N/A**
- **Location 5:** The critical threat range is **doubled** (e.g., 20 becomes 19-20, 19-20 becomes 17-20).
- **Location 6:** **N/A**
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The critical threat range is **tripled**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This effect does not stack with the *Keen* weapon property or the *Improved Critical* feat.
- (Note: Skareggrún is a more advanced version of this rune).

---

## **Visual**
A single, sharp vertical line that seems to shimmer with a cold blue light.

---

## **The Legend of the Keen Edge**
- The most basic rune taught to every dwarven apprentice, representing the first step in mastering the art of the runic forge.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Various:** A universal design used by almost every dwarven smith since the dawn of time.
