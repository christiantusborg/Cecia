# Rune of Widow’s Web

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ekkja** (Dwarven for 'Widow')
- **Vef** (Dwarven for 'Web')
> Meaning: "The Weaver's Insight"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants the user enhanced reflexes and the ability to predict an enemy’s movements in combat, much like a spider sensing vibrations on its web.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wielder gains a **+1 insight bonus** to Armor Class.
- **Location 2:** The wielder gains a **+2 bonus** on Initiative checks.
- **Location 3:** The wielder can use *Combat Reflexes* (if they have it) to make one additional attack of opportunity per round.
- **Location 4:** Insight bonus to AC increases to **+3**.
- **Location 5:** The wielder gains the *Uncanny Dodge* ability while the weapon is drawn.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wielder can invoke the rune to gain the effects of the *Haste* spell for 3 rounds.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The wielder can never be caught flat-footed and gains a **+5 bonus** to AC against attacks of opportunity.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- The wielder must maintain a high state of focus; the bonuses are lost if the wielder is stunned or paralyzed.

---

## **Visual**
A delicate, intricate web pattern that spreads across the crossguard and hilt of the weapon.

---

## **The Legend of the Widow’s Web**
- Inspired by the **Great Spiders of the Deeps**, which the dwarves of the lower tunnels learned to respect for their uncanny awareness.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Arachne Ironweaver:** A female dwarf who lived among the cave spiders and claimed they taught her how to "read the air."
