# Rune of Gravity Well

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Thungr** (Dwarven for 'Heavy/Weight')
- **Brunnr** (Dwarven for 'Well/Spring')
> Meaning: "The Spring of Weight"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The sword can create a localized gravitational field, making it difficult for enemies to move or escape.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, you can create a 5-foot radius area around the target of a hit that is treated as **Difficult Terrain** for 1 round.
- **Location 2:** Radius increases to **10 feet**.
- **Location 3:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 4:** Creatures in the area must succeed on a **DC 16 Strength save** or be pulled 5 feet toward the center.
- **Location 5:** Gravity is so intense that flying creatures in the area are pulled to the ground.
- **Location 6:** Radius increases to **20 feet**. Save DC increases to **20**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The wielder is immune to the gravity, and can use it to "anchor" themselves, becoming immune to being moved against their will.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Effects last for a number of rounds equal to the slot level.

---

## **Visual**
A dark, dense rune that seems to absorb light. When activated, the air around the weapon warps slightly.

---

## **The Legend of the Gravity Well**
- Used to trap agile elven scouts during the **Border Skirmishes**, forcing them into close-quarters combat where the dwarves excelled.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Magni the Heavy:** A smith known for using incredibly heavy hammers and having a deep fascination with the weight of the world.
