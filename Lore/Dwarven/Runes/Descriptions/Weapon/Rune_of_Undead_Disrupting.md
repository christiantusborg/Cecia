# Rune of the Undead (Disrupting)

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Dauðr** (Dwarven for 'Dead')
- **Slíta** (Dwarven for 'Disrupt/Break')
> Meaning: "The Death-Breaker"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
On a successful hit against undead, the sword disrupts their negative energy essence, potentially destroying them instantly.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The weapon deals an extra **1d6 positive energy damage** to undead.
- **Location 2:** Damage increases to **2d6**.
- **Location 3:** On a hit, an undead creature must succeed on a **DC 14 Will save** or be *Turned* (as per the cleric ability).
- **Location 4:** Damage increases to **3d6**. Any undead creature struck must succeed on a **DC 15 Will save** or be destroyed instantly.
- **Location 5:** Save DC increases to **18**. The weapon ignores the Damage Reduction of undead.
- **Location 6:** Damage increases to **5d6**. Save DC increases to **22**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The weapon emits a constant 20-foot aura of *Consecrate*.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Only affects creatures with the [Undead] type.

---

## **Visual**
A blindingly bright sun-like rune that seems to pulse with a holy warmth.

---

## **The Legend of the Undead Disrupting**
- Forged by the **Clerics of the High Peak** to cleanse the catacombs of the lich-kings.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **High Priest Thrum:** A holy warrior who believed that every undead was an insult to the cycle of life and stone.
