# Rune of Narasimha's Wrath

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Narasimha** (Foreign name for the Lion-Man avatar)
- **Reiði** (Dwarven for 'Wrath')
> Meaning: "The Wrath of the Lion-Man"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The wielder channels a savage, primal fury that manifests as spectral claws and a roar that can paralyze foes.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** On a critical hit, the wielder emits a roar. The target must succeed on a **DC 15 Will save** or be *Shaken*.
- **Location 2:** The weapon deals an extra **1d8 slashing damage** (spectral claws).
- **Location 3:** The wielder gains a **+2 bonus** on Strength checks and Strength-based skills.
- **Location 4:** Roar save DC increases to **18**. Target is *Frightened* on a failure.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wielder can enter a "Narasimha Fury," gaining the *Rage* ability for 5 rounds.
- **Location 6:** The spectral claws deal **2d8 damage** and have a threat range of 19-20.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The wielder can bypass any "invulnerability" or Damage Reduction of a specific target for 1 round once per day.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is particularly effective against "un-killable" foes or those with high DR.

---

## **Visual**
A lion's head with a mane of flames. The rune glows with a fierce orange-red light.

---

## **The Legend of Narasimha's Wrath**
- Named after a deity who appeared as a man-lion to destroy a demon-king who could not be killed by any man or beast.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Vikram of the Iron Forge:** A smith who specialized in "impossible" weapons for "impossible" tasks.
