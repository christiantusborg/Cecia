# Rune of the Arcane Archer

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Galdr** (Dwarven for 'Magic/Arcane')
- **Bogmaðr** (Dwarven for 'Archer')
> Meaning: "The Magic-Shooter's Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Designed for ranged weapons, this rune allows the wielder's shots to curve around obstacles or ignore magical cover.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer's ranged attacks ignore **half cover**.
- **Location 2:** Ranged attacks ignore **total cover** if the wielder knows the target's location (though the 50% miss chance still applies).
- **Location 3:** The wielder gains a **+1 bonus** on attack rolls with ranged weapons.
- **Location 4:** Ranged attacks ignore **total cover** completely (no miss chance) as the arrows/bolts curve magically.
- **Location 5:** The wielder can imbue one arrow/bolt per day with an area spell they know (as per the *Imbue Arrow* ability).
- **Location 6:** Ranged attacks ignore **displacement** and **mirror image** effects.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] For bows/crossbows, the wielder can fire a "Ghost Arrow" that passes through solid objects to strike a target on the other side.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is typically applied to bows, crossbows, or the bracers of an archer (if treated as a "body" rune, but here listed as weapon).

---

## **Visual**
An eye with an arrow passing through the pupil, glowing with a sharp emerald light.

---

## **The Legend of the Arcane Archer**
- Forged for the **Elven-Dwarven Alliance** to allow their combined forces to strike from behind barricades.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Sylvarius Half-Dwarf:** A smith of mixed heritage who wanted to combine the elven grace of archery with dwarven runic power.
