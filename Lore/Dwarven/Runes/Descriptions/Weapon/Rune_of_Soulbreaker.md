# Rune of Soulbreaker

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sál** (Dwarven for 'Soul')
- **Brjóta** (Dwarven for 'Break')
> Meaning: "The Soul-Shatterer"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune targets the psychological and spiritual stability of the target, inflicting intense fear and spiritual fatigue.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** On a hit, the target must succeed on a **DC 14 Will save** or be *Shaken* for 1d4 rounds.
- **Location 2:** Save DC increases to **16**.
- **Location 3:** On a critical hit, the target is *Frightened* for 1 round (Will DC 18).
- **Location 4:** Save DC increases to **20**. The fear duration increases to 1 round on a normal hit.
- **Location 5:** The weapon deals an extra **1d4 Wisdom damage** on a critical hit.
- **Location 6:** On a critical hit, the target is *Cowered* for 1 round (Will DC 22).
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The blade's aura is so terrifying that any creature within 30 feet must succeed on a **DC 20 Will save** or be *Shaken* just by the weapon being drawn.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Mind-affecting fear effect.

---

## **Visual**
A fractured, jagged rune that looks like a cracked mirror. It emits a faint, high-pitched ringing sound when it strikes.

---

## **The Legend of the Soulbreaker**
- Used by the **Dread-Knights** to break the morale of enemy armies before the first blow was even struck.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Malakor the Grim:** A smith who studied the psychology of terror to create the ultimate weapon of psychological warfare.
