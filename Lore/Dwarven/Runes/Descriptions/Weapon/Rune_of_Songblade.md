# Rune of Songblade

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Galdr** (Dwarven for 'Song/Chant')
- **Blóð** (Dwarven for 'Blade/Blood')
> Meaning: "The Singing Blade"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The sword sings an eerie, resonant melody when drawn, affecting the emotions and concentration of those who hear it.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** While drawn, all enemies within 10 feet take a **-1 penalty** on saves against Fear effects.
- **Location 2:** Penalty increases to **-2**.
- **Location 3:** Enemies within 20 feet must succeed on a **DC 14 Will save** or be *Shaken* for as long as they hear the song.
- **Location 4:** The wielder gains a **+2 morale bonus** on attack rolls while the blade sings.
- **Location 5:** The song can be used to *Counterstrong* (as the bard ability) once per day.
- **Location 6:** The song becomes a *Dirge of Doom*, causing enemies to become *Frightened* (Will DC 18).
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The blade can perform a *Song of Discord*, forcing enemies to attack each other.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- The effect is sonic and mind-affecting.

---

## **Visual**
A series of flowing, musical-staff-like lines that seem to vibrate even when the sword is still.

---

## **The Legend of the Songblade**
- Legend says the first Songblade was forged by a dwarf who was raised by bards and wanted to combine his two passions.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Luthier Ironvoice:** A rare dwarven bard-smith who treated every hammer strike as a musical note.
