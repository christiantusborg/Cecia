# Rune of the Giant Slayer

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Jötunn** (Dwarven for 'Giant')
- **Bani** (Dwarven for 'Slayer/Bane')
> Meaning: "The Bane of Giants"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The weapon is particularly effective against creatures of Large size or larger, dealing significant bonus damage.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Deals an extra **1d6 damage** against Large or larger creatures.
- **Location 2:** Damage increases to **2d6**.
- **Location 3:** The weapon is treated as one size category larger for determining damage against such creatures.
- **Location 4:** Damage increases to **3d6**.
- **Location 5:** On a successful hit, the giant must succeed on a **DC 18 Fortitude save** or have its movement speed halved for 1 round.
- **Location 6:** Damage increases to **4d6**. The weapon's critical threat range is doubled against giants.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] If the target is a Giant, a critical hit forces a **DC 25 Fortitude save** or the target is slain instantly.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- "Giant" includes any creature with the Giant type or any humanoid/monstrous humanoid of Large size or larger.

---

## **Visual**
A vertical rune with several horizontal bars across it, resembling a ladder or a tall figure being toppled.

---

## **The Legend of the Giant Slayer**
- Forged during the **War of the Peaks**, where a handful of dwarves held back a hill giant army using weapons bearing this rune.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Durgrim Stonefist:** A legendary warrior-smith who was famously short, even for a dwarf, and held a personal grudge against anything taller than six feet.
