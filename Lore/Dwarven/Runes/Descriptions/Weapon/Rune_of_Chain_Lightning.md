# Rune of Chain Lightning

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Keðja** (Dwarven for 'Chain')
- **Lýsing** (Dwarven for 'Lightning/Light')
> Meaning: "The Linked Lightning"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The sword can unleash a chain lightning bolt that arcs between multiple targets upon a successful hit.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, on a hit, a spark arcs to 1 secondary target within 15 feet, dealing **1d6 electricity damage** (Reflex DC 14 for half).
- **Location 2:** Damage increases to **2d6**.
- **Location 3:** Arcs to **2 targets**.
- **Location 4:** Damage increases to **3d6**. Save DC increases to **17**.
- **Location 5:** Arcs to **3 targets**. Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 6:** Damage increases to **5d6**. Save DC increases to **20**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] On every critical hit, a *Chain Lightning* spell (caster level 11) is triggered automatically.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Secondary targets must be within 15 feet of the primary or previous target.

---

## **Visual**
A zigzagging rune that glows with a brilliant, crackling blue-white light. Small sparks occasionally jump between the rune and the wielder's fingers.

---

## **The Legend of the Chain Lightning**
- Forged by the **Cloud-Forgers** of the high peaks, who harvested lightning during summer storms to temper their blades.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Eitri Stormcaller:** A dwarf who was struck by lightning three times and claimed it gave him "clarity of craft."
