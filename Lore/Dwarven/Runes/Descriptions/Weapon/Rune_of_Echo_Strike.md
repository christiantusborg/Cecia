# Rune of Echo Strike

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Omsyn** (Dwarven for 'Echo/Repeat')
- **Hjör** (Dwarven for 'Sword/Blade')
> Meaning: "The Repeating Blade"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The sword can "remember" a successful strike and repeat the impact a moment later, essentially doubling the effectiveness of an attack.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, as a swift action after a successful melee attack, you can deal half the weapon damage again to the same target.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** The "echo" damage is now equal to the full weapon damage (excluding bonus dice).
- **Location 4:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The echo now includes all bonus damage dice (sneak attack, elemental damage, etc.).
- **Location 6:** Once per encounter, you can trigger a "Continuous Echo," repeating the damage every round for 3 rounds.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] Every successful strike automatically generates an echo dealing 25% damage.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- The echo occurs at the end of the wielder's turn.

---

## **Visual**
A series of concentric circles that seem to ripple across the blade's surface like water.

---

## **The Legend of the Echo Strike**
- Discovered by **Snorri the Swift**, who noticed that certain frequencies of dwarven steel would vibrate in a way that mimicked previous impacts.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Snorri the Swift:** A master of acoustic magic and metallurgy who believed that time was just another medium, like metal, that could be shaped.
