# Rune of Kvöl-Rún (Rune of Infinite Agony)

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Kvöl** (Old Dwarven for 'Torture' or 'Agony')
- **Rún** (Dwarven for 'Rune' or 'Mystery')
> Meaning: "The Mark of Eternal Suffering"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Upon a successful strike, the rune dredges up the target's most traumatic memories and manifests them as physical agony. This pain is permanent and does not fade with time, resting only when the target is magically restored.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+25 (Legendary Tier)**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Legendary** (Forbidden by every Dwarven Council in Cecia)

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
*Note: All effects require a **Fortitude Save (DC 15 + 1/2 Wielder's Level + Charisma Modifier)**. On a failed save, the effect is **PERMANENT** until a Restoration, Greater Restoration, or Heal spell is cast.*

- **Location 1:** **Sickened.** The target suffers a constant -2 penalty on all attack rolls, saves, and skill checks.
- **Location 2:** **Agonized.** The penalty increases to -4 on all attack rolls, saves, and skill checks. 
- **Location 3:** **Fatigued.** The target is permanently Fatigued. If they were already Fatigued, they become Exhausted.
- **Location 4:** **Exhausted.** The target is permanently Exhausted. They move at half speed and suffer -6 to Strength and Dexterity.
- **Location 5:** **Staggered.** The pain is so intense the target can only take a single Move or Standard action each round.
- **Location 6:** **Nauseated.** The target is in such white-hot agony they can only take a single Move action each round and are incapable of attacking or casting spells.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] **Fractured Will.** The target's soul is locked in a loop of its worst memory. They are treated as **Nauseated** (can only take a single Move action) and their **speed is halved**. They cannot attack, cast spells, or perform complex actions, and they suffer a **-6 penalty** to AC as they stumble blindly through their own mental nightmare.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- **Memory Trigger:** If the target has a specific trauma in their backstory, the save DC increases by +2 as the rune feeds on the existing rift in their spirit.
- **Irremovable:** This effect cannot be removed by rest, mundane medicine, or *Lesser Restoration*. Only *Restoration* or higher magic can mend the spiritual wound.

#### **4.4.1 Roleplay Rules (Optional)**
- **The Screaming Steel:** When drawn, the blade emits a sound like a distant, echoing scream. 
- **Tainted Soul:** A wielder who carries this rune for more than a year begins to lose the ability to feel empathy, their heart turning to cold, unyielding iron.

#### **4.4.2 Critical Hit Effects (Optional)**
- **Nerve Shatter:** On a critical hit, the target receives **no saving throw**. The agony takes hold instantly and completely.

## **Visual**
The rune is a series of overlapping, jagged hooks that appear to be digging into the surface of the blade. It glows with a sickly, bruised-purple light that flickers like a dying candle. When it strikes, a thin trail of black smoke—the victim's fading resolve—rises from the wound.

---

## **The Legend of the Kvöl-Rún**
- Forged by **Varek the Unbroken**, a runesmith who was held captive for thirty years in the pits of the Underdark. He didn't forge the rune with a hammer; he forged it with the memory of every lash he ever received. When he finally escaped, he used the first Kvöl-blade to walk through his captors' fortress like a god of sorrow.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Varek the Unbroken:** A dwarf whose body was a map of scars but whose mind was a fortress of spite. He believed that death was a mercy his enemies didn't deserve. He vanished shortly after his revenge, leaving his scrolls hidden in a vault that can only be opened by a drop of blood shed in true despair.
