# Rune of Morphing

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Móta** (Dwarven for 'Shape/Form')
- **Hamskifti** (Dwarven for 'Change')
> Meaning: "The Shape-Shifter's Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune allows the weapon to transform into any other type of weapon at the wielder's command.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** As a standard action, the weapon can transform into any other simple or martial weapon of the same size.
- **Location 2:** Transformation is now a **move action**.
- **Location 3:** The weapon can transform into two light weapons (if it was a one-handed or two-handed weapon).
- **Location 4:** Transformation is now a **swift action**.
- **Location 5:** The weapon can transform into any exotic weapon the wielder is proficient with.
- **Location 6:** Transformation is now a **free action** once per turn.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] The weapon can transform into complex mechanical objects, like a repeating crossbow or a specialized tool.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- The weapon retains its enhancement bonuses and other runes in its new form.
- Weight and damage are adjusted to match the new weapon type.

---

## **Visual**
A fluid, mercury-like rune that seems to shift and shimmer even when the blade is static.

---

## **The Legend of the Morphing Rune**
- Created by **Varick the Versatile**, a mercenary who hated carrying a heavy arsenal and wanted one tool for every job.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Varick the Versatile:** A smith-adventurer who valued flexibility and adaptation above all else.
