# Rune of Ljósvaki-Rún (Blinding Light)

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ljós** (Dwarven for 'Light')
- **Vaki** (Dwarven for 'Awakening' or 'Wake')
> Meaning: "The Awakening of Light"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
This rune allows a weapon to emit a brilliant radiance upon command or impact, ranging from a steady glow to a blinding, sun-like flash that sears the eyes of all who behold it.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
*Note: Unless otherwise stated, all effects are triggered by a command word or upon a successful melee strike. Saving throw DCs are **10 + 1/2 Wielder's Level + Charisma Modifier**.*

- **Location 1:** **Lesser Radiance.** The weapon sheds light as per the *Light* spell (20-foot radius of bright light, 20-foot radius of dim light) for 10 minutes per wielder level.
- **Location 2:** **Greater Radiance.** As Location 1, but the light is as bright as a *Daylight* spell and the duration is doubled (20 minutes per level).
- **Location 3:** **Blinding Flash.** Upon a successful strike, the target must succeed on a **Reflex Save** or be **Blinded** for 1d4 rounds.
- **Location 4:** **Stunning Brilliance.** As Location 3, but the target is also **Dazed** for 1 round on a failed save from the sheer shock of the light.
- **Location 5:** **Solar Flare.** The flash now affects all enemies within a 10-foot radius of the struck target. All must make a **Reflex Save** or be **Blinded** for 1d6 rounds.
- **Location 6:** **Searing Light.** The flash deals an additional **3d6 points of damage** to undead or creatures sensitive to light (such as those with Light Sensitivity or Light Vulnerability) on top of the blinding effect.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only] **Sunburst Strike.** Once per encounter, upon a successful strike, the weapon releases a burst of light equivalent to a *Sunburst* spell. All creatures within a 80-foot radius take **6d6 points of damage** (undead take **1d6 per wielder level, max 25d6**) and are **permanently Blinded**. A successful **Reflex Save** halves the damage and negates the blindness.

---

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- **Light Sensitivity:** Creatures with Light Sensitivity suffer a -2 penalty on their saving throws against this rune's effects.
- **Cooldown:** The blinding effects (Locations 3–6) can only be triggered once per round. The Sunburst Strike (Location 7) is once per encounter.

#### **4.4.1 Roleplay Rules (Optional)**
- **Beacon of Hope:** Wielders of this rune often find themselves being looked to as leaders or symbols of hope in dark places.

#### **4.4.2 Critical Hit Effects (Optional)**
- **Blinding Critical:** On a critical hit, the duration of any blindness effect is doubled, and the target receives no saving throw if the rune is in Slot 3 or higher.

---

## **Visual**
The rune consists of a central circle with eight lines radiating outwards like a stylized sun. It glows with a pure, brilliant white light that seems to push back even the deepest shadows. When it strikes, the point of impact flares with a intensity that leaves "ghost images" in the vision of anyone nearby.

---

## **The Legend of the Ljósvaki-Rún**
- Forged by **Elora Sun-Seeker**, a dwarven smith who was born blind but regained her sight through a vision of the first dawn. She spent her life tryin' to capture that first, perfect light in steel, believin' that darkness was just a lack of courage.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Elora Sun-Seeker:** A dwarf who always kept her forge open to the sky, even in the middle of winter. She was known for her kindness and her refusal to work in the dark, claimin' that "true craft requires the truth of light."
