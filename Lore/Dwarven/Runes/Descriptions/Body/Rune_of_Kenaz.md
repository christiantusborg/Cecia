# Rune of Kenaz

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Kenaz** (The rune for 'Torch/Knowledge/Craft')
> Meaning: "The Torch of Knowledge"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants a supernatural boost to the wearer's Intelligence and intellectual focus.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 enhancement bonus to Intelligence**.
- **Location 2:** **N/A**
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Search and Knowledge checks.
- **Location 4:** **N/A**
- **Location 5:** The enhancement bonus to Intelligence increases to **+4**.
- **Location 6:** **N/A**
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The enhancement bonus to Intelligence increases to **+6**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is highly valued by wizards, tacticians, and master-smiths.

---

## **Visual**
The Kenaz rune (resembling a backward 'C' or an angled torch flame), glowing with a bright orange-yellow light.

---

## **The Legend of Kenaz**
- Legend says it was first gifted to the dwarves by the **God of the Forge** to allow them to understand the secrets of the elements.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **The First Smith:** A mythical figure who is said to have forged the world itself.
