# Rune of Uruz

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Uruz** (The rune for 'Ox/Strength')
> Meaning: "The Strength of the Wild Ox"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants a supernatural boost to the wearer's physical strength.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 enhancement bonus to Strength**.
- **Location 2:** **N/A**
- **Location 3:** The wearer's carrying capacity is doubled.
- **Location 4:** **N/A**
- **Location 5:** The enhancement bonus to Strength increases to **+4**.
- **Location 6:** **N/A**
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The enhancement bonus to Strength increases to **+6**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Does not stack with other enhancement bonuses to Strength (e.g., *Bull's Strength*).

---

## **Visual**
The Uruz rune (looks like an inverted 'U' or an ox's horn), glowing with a deep primal red light.

---

## **The Legend of Uruz**
- Based on the ancient runic alphabet of the first dwarves, representing the raw power of the earth.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Ancient Runesmiths:** A design passed down through generations of dwarven masters.
