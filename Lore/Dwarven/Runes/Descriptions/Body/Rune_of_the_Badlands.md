# Rune of the Badlands

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vondr** (Dwarven for 'Bad/Harsh')
- **Land** (Dwarven for 'Land/Earth')
> Meaning: "The Survival of the Harsh Earth"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Enhances survival skills, such as finding water or shelter in the wastelands, and providing resistance to harsh environments.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on Survival checks to find food and water.
- **Location 2:** The wearer is immune to the effects of *Extreme Heat* and *Extreme Cold*.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can find their way in any terrain, gaining a **+2 bonus** on Knowledge (Geography) checks.
- **Location 4:** The wearer only needs half the normal amount of food and water to survive.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on saves against poison and disease.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Create Food and Water*.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can survive in any environment, including underwater or in a vacuum, for up to 1 hour per day.

### **4.3 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is standard for explorers and desert-dwellers.

---

## **Visual**
A dry, cracked earth pattern etched in a deep terracotta orange.

---

## **The Legend of the Badlands**
- Created for the **Wanderers of the Dust**, who lived in the harshest deserts of Cecia.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Nomad Kael:** A dwarf who chose to live under the sun and learned its secrets of survival.
