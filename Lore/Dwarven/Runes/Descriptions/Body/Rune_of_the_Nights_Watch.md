# Rune of the Night's Watch

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Nótt** (Dwarven for 'Night')
- **Vaka** (Dwarven for 'Watch/Vigil')
> Meaning: "The Vigil in the Dark"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Strengthens bonds and loyalty among a group, providing bonuses when fighting alongside allies.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+1 morale bonus** on attack and damage rolls when adjacent to at least one ally.
- **Location 2:** Bonus increases to **+2**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can use *Help an Ally* as a swift action once per day.
- **Location 4:** The wearer is immune to **Fear** as long as they are within 30 feet of at least two allies.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can grant all allies within 30 feet a **+2 bonus** on saves.
- **Location 6:** The morale bonus increases to **+4**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer and all allies within 60 feet share their lowest saving throw bonus with each other.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is foundational for military units and adventuring parties.

---

## **Visual**
A shield with a crescent moon above it, glowing with a steady silver-blue light.

---

## **The Legend of the Night's Watch**
- Created for the **Sentinels of the Outer Gates**, who stood guard through the longest winters.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Commander Alfrid:** A legendary military leader who understood that a group's strength was in its unity.
