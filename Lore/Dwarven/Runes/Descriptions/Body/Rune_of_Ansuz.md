# Rune of Ansuz

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ansuz** (The rune for 'Ancestors/God/Communication')
> Meaning: "The Voice of the Ancestors"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants a boost to the wearer's Charisma and presence, allowing them to lead with greater authority.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 enhancement bonus to Charisma**.
- **Location 2:** **N/A**
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Diplomacy and Intimidate checks.
- **Location 4:** **N/A**
- **Location 5:** The enhancement bonus to Charisma increases to **+4**.
- **Location 6:** **N/A**
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The enhancement bonus to Charisma increases to **+6**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the armor of kings and diplomats.

---

## **Visual**
The Ansuz rune (resembling a stylized face or a banner), glowing with a brilliant golden light.

---

## **The Legend of Ansuz**
- Legend says it allows the wearer to hear the whispers of their ancestors, guiding their words.
