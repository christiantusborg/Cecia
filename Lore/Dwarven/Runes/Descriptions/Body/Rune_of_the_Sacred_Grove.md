# Rune of the Sacred Grove

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Heilagr** (Dwarven for 'Sacred')
- **Lúndr** (Dwarven for 'Grove')
> Meaning: "The Protection of the Sacred Grove"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Offers protection against dark magic and provides a sense of peace and sanctuary.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** on saves against spells with the [Evil] or [Necromancy] descriptors.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Will saves against mind-affecting effects.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can cast *Sanctuary* once per day.
- **Location 4:** Bonus to saves increases to **+3**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Dispel Magic*.
- **Location 6:** The wearer is immune to **Curse** effects.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of a *Magic Circle against Evil* spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the armor of paladins and druids.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized tree with glowing silver leaves.

---

## **The Legend of the Sacred Grove**
- Forged for the **Protectors of the Council Glade** to ensure they could not be corrupted.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **High Druid Elara:** A female dwarf who lived among the surface elves and learned their secrets of magic.
