# Rune of the Faceless Man

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Andlitlaus** (Dwarven for 'Faceless')
- **Maðr** (Dwarven for 'Man')
> Meaning: "The Rune of the Unknown Face"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Allows a temporary change in appearance or grants enhanced stealth abilities, making the wearer nearly impossible to identify.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer can cast *Disguise Self* once per day.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on Disguise checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer's alignment and true nature are hidden from magical detection (as per *Undetectable Alignment*).
- **Location 4:** Usage of *Disguise Self* increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer can cast *Alter Self* once per day.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains a **+5 bonus** on Stealth checks while in a crowd.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can adopt the appearance and voice of a specific person they have touched for 24 hours.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is the hallmark of secret agents and master thieves.

---

## **Visual**
A blank, oval face with no features, glowing with a soft grey light.

---

## **The Legend of the Faceless Man**
- Created by the **Shadow-Guilds** of the surface cities to allow their agents to move unseen through society.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **No-One:** A smith whose identity was never known and whose workshop was never found.
