# Rune of Nauthiz

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Nauthiz** (The rune for 'Need/Distress/Constraint')
> Meaning: "The Constraint of the Oath"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
A version of a Geas/Quest rune that is non-forcing but will inflict severe penalties on the wearer for breaking their oath.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer can set a specific "Oath" (e.g., "I will protect the King"). If they break it, they take a **-2 penalty** to all stats for 24 hours.
- **Location 2:** Penalty increases to **-4**.
- **Location 3:** If the oath is broken, the wearer takes **1d6 damage** per hour until the oath is restored.
- **Location 4:** The penalty increases to **-6**.
- **Location 5:** If the oath is broken, the wearer is *Sickened* until they complete a task of atonement.
- **Location 6:** The penalty increases to **-8**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] If the wearer breaks their oath, they must succeed on a **DC 25 Fortitude save** or be reduced to 0 HP instantly.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- The "Oath" must be clearly defined and agreed upon when the rune is applied.

---

## **Visual**
The Nauthiz rune (resembling a cross-bar on a vertical line), glowing with a cold, dull blue light.

---

## **The Legend of Nauthiz**
- Used by the **Order of the Silver Oaths** to ensure the absolute loyalty of its members.

---

## **Description of Creator**
