# Rune of Zanek

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Zanek** (Dwarven interpretation of 'Wisdom/Vision')
> Meaning: "The Rune of the Inner Eye"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants a supernatural boost to the wearer's Wisdom and spiritual awareness.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 enhancement bonus to Wisdom**.
- **Location 2:** **N/A**
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Sense Motive and Listen checks.
- **Location 4:** **N/A**
- **Location 5:** The enhancement bonus to Wisdom increases to **+4**.
- **Location 6:** **N/A**
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The enhancement bonus to Wisdom increases to **+6**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the armor of priests and wise-men.

---

## **Visual**
A series of concentric circles glowing with a soft, ethereal violet light.

---

## **The Legend of Zanek**
- Created for the **Elders of the High Council** to allow them to see the truth in all things.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Seer Thalia:** A female dwarf who was famously wise and believed that "the heart knows what the eyes cannot see."
