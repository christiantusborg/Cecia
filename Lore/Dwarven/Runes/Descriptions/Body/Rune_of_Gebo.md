# Rune of Gebo

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Gebo** (The rune for 'Gift/Partnership')
> Meaning: "The Gift of the Mind-Link"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Creates a mind link between two characters, allowing for seamless communication and coordination.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer can establish a mental link with one willing creature within 30 feet once per day for 1 hour.
- **Location 2:** The link range increases to 60 feet.
- **Location 3:** The link allows the sharing of sensory information (sight, sound).
- **Location 4:** Usage of the link increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The link can be maintained over any distance on the same plane.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the linked characters can "swap" a single successful save result.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The link is permanent and allows the linked pair to cast *Telepathic Bond* at will.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Requires both characters to be wearing a piece of equipment with the Gebo rune to establish the link.

---

## **Visual**
The Gebo rune (resembling an 'X'), glowing with a warm pink light.

---

## **The Legend of Gebo**
- Legend says it was first used by the **Twin Kings of Lagon** to lead their armies as one mind.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Master Alfrid:** A smith who specialized in diplomatic and relationship-based magic.
