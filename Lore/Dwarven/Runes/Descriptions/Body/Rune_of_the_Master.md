# Rune of the Master

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Meistari** (Dwarven for 'Master')
- **Gefa** (Dwarven for 'Skill/Gift')
> Meaning: "The Master's Touch"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Boosts the ability to master a skill, granting bonuses to specific skill checks and professional tasks.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer chooses one skill; they gain a **+2 bonus** on checks with that skill.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** on all *Professional* and *Craft* skill checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can choose a second skill; they gain a **+2 bonus** on that skill as well.
- **Location 4:** The wearer can *Take 10* on their chosen skills even in stressful situations.
- **Location 5:** Bonus to chosen skills increases to **+4**.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can *Take 20* on a chosen skill check.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is considered "Trained" in all skills, regardless of whether they have ranks in them.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Chosen skills must be selected when the rune is first applied and cannot be changed.

---

## **Visual**
A hand holding a hammer and a quill, glowing with a soft, steady gold light.

---

## **The Legend of the Master**
- Created for the **Master-Artisans of the Grand Forge** to ensure their work was always perfect.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **High Artisan Dain:** A smith who was famously precise and believed that "perfection is the only acceptable result."
