# Rune of the Ethereal Jaunt

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Andi** (Dwarven for 'Spirit/Spirit-world')
- **Fara** (Dwarven for 'Travel/Go')
> Meaning: "The Spirit-Traveler's Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Once per day, the wearer can become ethereal, allowing them to pass through solid objects or avoid attacks for a brief moment.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per long rest, the wearer can become ethereal for **1 round** (as per the *Ethereal Jaunt* spell).
- **Location 2:** Duration increases to **1d3 rounds**.
- **Location 3:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 4:** The wearer can take one other willing creature with them into the Ethereal Plane.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can use *Blink* for 1 minute instead of *Ethereal Jaunt*.
- **Location 6:** Duration of *Ethereal Jaunt* increases to **1 minute**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can phase in and out of the Ethereal Plane as a move action at will.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- While ethereal, you can only be harmed by other ethereal creatures or force effects.

---

## **Visual**
A shifting, semi-transparent rune that seems to exist partially "inside" the metal of the armor.

---

## **The Legend of the Ethereal Jaunt**
- Discovered by **Snorri the Ghost-Smith**, who accidentally entered the Ethereal Plane while quenching a blade in a pool of spirit-essence.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Snorri the Ghost-Smith:** A mystic who claimed that the "real" world was just a shadow of the spirit world.
