# Rune of Raidho

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Raidho** (The rune for 'Ride/Travel/Journey')
> Meaning: "The Speed of the Journey"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Used to enhance movement, granting additional speed and the ability to act with supernatural haste.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, the wearer can gain the effects of the *Haste* spell for 1 round.
- **Location 2:** The wearer's base land speed increases by **5 feet**.
- **Location 3:** Usage of *Haste* increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 4:** Base land speed increases by **10 feet**.
- **Location 5:** Usage of *Haste* increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can use *Haste* for 1 minute instead of 1 round.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of the *Longstrider* spell and can use *Haste* 5/day.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is favored by scouts and cavalry.

---

## **Visual**
The Raidho rune (resembling an 'R' or a wheel), glowing with a bright white light that seems to trail behind the wearer.

---

## **The Legend of Raidho**
- Created for the **Pony-Riders of Lagon** to allow them to outrun any foe.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Gimli the Swift:** A smith who loved the wind in his beard and the feel of the open road.
