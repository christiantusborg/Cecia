# Rune of Baron’s Favor

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Barun** (Dwarven for 'Baron/Noble')
- **Gefa** (Dwarven for 'Favor/Gift')
> Meaning: "The Noble's Influence"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Provides protection or boosts charisma, allowing for better negotiation or diplomacy, and marking the wearer as a person of importance.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Diplomacy and Intimidate checks.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** on Charisma-based skill checks.
- **Location 3:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Enthrall*.
- **Location 4:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Charisma.
- **Location 5:** The wearer is immune to the *Fear* condition while in a noble court.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Suggestion*.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer's Charisma increases by **+4** and they gain a **+10 bonus** to Diplomacy checks.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is popular among diplomats, politicians, and high-ranking military officers.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized crown with two crossed scepters, glowing with a rich golden light.

---

## **The Legend of the Baron’s Favor**
- First forged for the **Ambassadors of the Kingdom of Lagon** to ensure their words were always heard.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Master Alfrid:** A smith who spent more time in the royal courts than in his own forge.
