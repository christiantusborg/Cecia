# Rune of the Relentless

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Hraustr** (Dwarven for 'Relentless/Valiant')
- **Hlíf** (Dwarven for 'Defense/Armor')
> Meaning: "The Unyielding Heart"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants the wearer additional willpower to continue fighting even when below 0 hit points.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains the *Diehard* feat for free while wearing the armor.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on saves against death effects.
- **Location 3:** Once per day, if the wearer would be killed, they instead remain at **-9 HP** and are stable.
- **Location 4:** The wearer is immune to **Fear** effects while below half HP.
- **Location 5:** The wearer can act normally while between 0 and -9 HP without taking damage for acting.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Death Ward*.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can survive at up to **-20 HP** before dying.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This is standard for elite shock-troops.

---

## **Visual**
A solid, unbreaking bar with three blood-red drops below it.

---

## **The Legend of the Relentless**
- Forged for the **Berserkers of the High Peaks**, who were famous for fighting until the very end.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Bordin the Unyielding:** A smith who believed that a warrior's spirit was more important than their flesh.
