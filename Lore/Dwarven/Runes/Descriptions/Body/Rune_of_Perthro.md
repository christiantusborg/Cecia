# Rune of Perthro

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Perthro** (The rune for 'Luck/Dice/Mystery')
> Meaning: "The Glimpse of the Future"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants luck or a brief glimpse into the future, providing bonuses to defensive rolls and perception.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+1 luck bonus** on all saving throws.
- **Location 2:** Once per day, the wearer can reroll a failed save.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can use *Divination* once per day.
- **Location 4:** Luck bonus increases to **+2**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can choose to automatically succeed on a single skill check.
- **Location 6:** The wearer can see 1 round into the future, granting them a **+4 bonus to AC** for 1 round once per day.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can use the reroll ability **3/day**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This is a luck and divination effect.

---

## **Visual**
The Perthro rune (resembling a cup or a dice-shaker), glowing with a shifting, kaleidoscopic light.

---

## **The Legend of Perthro**
- Used by the **Gambler-Kings of the East** to ensure they always came out ahead.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Zane the Lucky:** A smith who was famously lucky and claimed he could "see the threads of fate."
