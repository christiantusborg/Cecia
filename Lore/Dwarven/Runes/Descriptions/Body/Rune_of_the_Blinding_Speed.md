# Rune of the Blinding Speed

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Blinda** (Dwarven for 'Blind')
- **Fljótr** (Dwarven for 'Swift')
> Meaning: "The Blindingly Swift Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Once per day, the wearer can move twice their normal speed without incurring attacks of opportunity, appearing as a blur of motion.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, as a swift action, the wearer can double their move speed for 1 round. This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** to AC against attacks of opportunity at all times.
- **Location 4:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer can cast *Blur* on themselves once per day for 1 minute.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can use the rune to gain the effects of the *Haste* spell for 3 rounds.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can use the double-speed effect as a move action twice per turn.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This is a transmutation effect.

---

## **Visual**
A series of overlapping lines that look like a motion blur, glowing with a shimmering silver light.

---

## **The Legend of the Blinding Speed**
- Legend tells of the **Wind-Runners of the Wastes**, who could cross a desert in a single night using this rune.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Zaria the Flash:** A female smith who was famously energetic and believed that "if you're not moving, you're dead."
