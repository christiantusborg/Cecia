# Rune of Berkano

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Berkano** (The rune for 'Birch/Fertility/Growth')
> Meaning: "The Rune of Empowerment"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Empowers fertility and growth, providing bonuses to health and vitality.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on saves against poison and disease.
- **Location 2:** The wearer heals naturally at **double the normal rate**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** on Constitution checks.
- **Location 4:** The wearer is immune to the effects of *Normal Aging* while wearing the armor.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can cast *Plant Growth*.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on saves against poison and disease.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer's maximum HP increases by **+10%**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the armor of midwives and farmers.

---

## **Visual**
The Berkano rune (resembling a 'B'), glowing with a soft green light.

---

## **The Legend of Berkano**
- Legend says it was first grown by a druid-smith who planted a magical birch in the center of his forge.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Flora the Gentle:** A female smith who believed that metal could be as nurturing as the earth.
