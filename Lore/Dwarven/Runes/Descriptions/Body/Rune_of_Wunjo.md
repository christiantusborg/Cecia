# Rune of Wunjo

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Wunjo** (The rune for 'Joy/Bliss/Success')
> Meaning: "The Joy of the Brave"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Provides a morale boost, making the wearer immune to fear and granting success in difficult tasks.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 morale bonus** on saves against fear effects.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+1 morale bonus** on attack rolls.
- **Location 3:** The wearer is immune to **Fear**.
- **Location 4:** Once per day, the wearer can reroll a failed skill check.
- **Location 5:** The morale bonus to attack rolls increases to **+2**.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains a **+4 morale bonus** on all saving throws for 1 minute once per day.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer and all allies within 30 feet are immune to fear and gain a **+2 morale bonus** on all rolls.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This is a mind-affecting morale effect.

---

## **Visual**
The Wunjo rune (resembling a 'P'), glowing with a bright golden-yellow light.

---

## **The Legend of Wunjo**
- Created for the **Victory-Feasts of Eisenwacht** to ensure the celebration never ended.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Gloin the Jolly:** A smith who was famous for his infectious laughter and his belief that joy was the best medicine.
