# Rune of Jera

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Jera** (The rune for 'Year/Harvest/Cycle')
> Meaning: "The Harvest of Life"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
A healing rune that provides a steady source of rejuvenation for the wearer.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, the wearer can activate the rune to heal **1d6 HP**.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** Healing increases to **2d6 HP**.
- **Location 4:** Usage increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains **Fast Healing 1** for 1 minute once per day.
- **Location 6:** Healing increases to **4d6 HP**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer gains **Fast Healing 2** permanently as long as the armor is worn.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This is a positive energy effect.

---

## **Visual**
The Jera rune (two interlocking 'V' shapes), glowing with a warm green-gold light.

---

## **The Legend of Jera**
- Created for the **Farming-Clans** to allow them to work in the fields from sunrise to sunset without getting sick.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Flora the Gentle:** A female smith who believed that metal could be as nurturing as the earth.
