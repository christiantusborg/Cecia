# Rune of Aleguard

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Öl** (Dwarven for 'Ale')
- **Vörðr** (Dwarven for 'Guard')
> Meaning: "The Guardian of the Ale"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Provides immunity to poisons, particularly from alcohol, and allows the wearer to drink without fear of intoxication.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+4 bonus** on saves against poison (especially alcohol-related).
- **Location 2:** The wearer is immune to **Intoxication**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can drink any amount of ale without suffering from hangovers or ill effects.
- **Location 4:** Bonus to poison saves increases to **+8**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can use *Neutralize Poison*.
- **Location 6:** The wearer is immune to **all poisons**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can turn any water they touch into high-quality dwarven ale.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Popular among dwarven tavern-goers and poison-hunters.

---

## **Visual**
A stylized mug with foam on top, glowing with a warm amber light.

---

## **The Legend of the Aleguard**
- Created for the **Great Feast of Lagon** to allow the king to celebrate for three days straight.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Master Brewer Bordin:** A smith who was more famous for his ale than his metalwork.
