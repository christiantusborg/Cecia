# Rune of the Swiftblade (Armor)

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Fljótr** (Dwarven for 'Swift')
- **Hlíf** (Dwarven for 'Armor/Shield')
> Meaning: "The Swift-Step Armor"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The armor becomes incredibly light and aerodynamically efficient, increasing the wearer's speed and agility.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+5**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Increases the wearer's base land speed by **5 feet**.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** on Initiative checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Reflex saves.
- **Location 4:** Base land speed increases by **10 feet**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *Dodge* feat for free.
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can move twice their normal speed for 1 round.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is always under the effect of the *Longstrider* spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is often found on the armor of light infantry and messengers.

---

## **Visual**
A series of sleek, parallel lines that seem to point forward, glowing with a faint white light.

---

## **The Legend of the Swiftblade**
- Created for the **Runners of the High Passes**, who needed to deliver messages across the peaks at incredible speeds.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Frey the Fast:** A smith who was famously thin and moved with an uncanny, airy grace.
