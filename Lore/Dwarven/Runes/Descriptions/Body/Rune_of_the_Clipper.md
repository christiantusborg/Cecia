# Rune of the Clipper

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sker** (Dwarven for 'Cut/Clip')
- **Fljótr** (Dwarven for 'Swift')
> Meaning: "The Swift Clipper"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Enhances martial prowess, granting improved agility and speed, allowing the wearer to move like a blur through the battlefield.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+15**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+1 bonus** to AC and Reflex saves.
- **Location 2:** The wearer's base land speed increases by **5 feet**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can take an extra **5-foot step** once per round.
- **Location 4:** The wearer gains the *Mobility* feat for free while wearing the armor.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can move at triple their speed for 1 round.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains the *Spring Attack* feat for free.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer can take an extra **Move Action** once per turn.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Favored by dual-wielders and light-armored duelists.

---

## **Visual**
A series of sharp, jagged lines that seem to cut through the air, glowing with a flickering white light.

---

## **The Legend of the Clipper**
- Created by **Elara the Swift-Stinger**, who could cut down an entire squadron before they even drew their swords.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Elara the Swift-Stinger:** A dwarf who was famously fast and believed that "if they can't see you, they can't hit you."
