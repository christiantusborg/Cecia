# Rune of Tiwaz

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Tiwaz** (The rune for 'Justice/Victory/The God Tyr')
> Meaning: "The Strike of Justice"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Provides bonuses in combat, granting the wearer a *True Strike* effect to ensure their next blow lands true.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** Once per day, the wearer can use *True Strike* as a move action.
- **Location 2:** Usage increases to **2/day**.
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+1 morale bonus** on attack rolls.
- **Location 4:** Usage of *True Strike* increases to **3/day**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can use *True Strike* as a swift action.
- **Location 6:** The morale bonus increases to **+2**.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer's first attack each round is always made with a **+2 bonus**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Favored by judges and those who seek to deliver divine justice.

---

## **Visual**
The Tiwaz rune (resembling an upward-pointing arrow), glowing with a sharp white-gold light.

---

## **The Legend of Tiwaz**
- Named after the **God of Victory**, who sacrificed his hand to bind a great evil.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Justice Thalric:** A smith who believed that every strike should be a blow for justice.
