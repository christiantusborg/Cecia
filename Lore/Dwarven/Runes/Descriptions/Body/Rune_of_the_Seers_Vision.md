# Rune of the Seer's Vision

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Spá** (Dwarven for 'Prophecy/Vision')
- **Sýn** (Dwarven for 'Sight')
> Meaning: "The Prophet's Sight"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The wearer gains enhanced vision, including darkvision and the ability to see through illusions or invisibility.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains **Darkvision up to 30 feet**. (If they already have it, it increases by 30 feet).
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Spot and Search checks.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can use *Detect Magic* at will.
- **Location 4:** Darkvision increases to **60 feet**.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *See Invisibility* ability for 1 minute once per day.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains a **+5 bonus** on saves against illusion effects.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of a *True Seeing* spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is popular among trackers and inquisitors.

---

## **Visual**
An eye with three pupils arranged in a triangle, glowing with a sharp white light.

---

## **The Legend of the Seer's Vision**
- Created by **Eyolf the Blind**, who sought a way to see the world "more clearly" after losing his physical sight in a duel.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Eyolf the Blind:** A smith who claimed that he could "feel" the shape of reality and translate it into metal.
