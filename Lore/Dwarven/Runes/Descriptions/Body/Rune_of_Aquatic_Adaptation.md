# Rune of Aquatic Adaptation

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vatn** (Dwarven for 'Water')
- **Bú** (Dwarven for 'Live/Dwell')
> Meaning: "The Water-Dweller's Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
The wearer can breathe underwater and gains a swimming speed equal to their walking speed.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer can breathe underwater for 10 minutes per day.
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **swimming speed** equal to half their walking speed.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can breathe underwater indefinitely.
- **Location 4:** The wearer gains a swimming speed equal to their full walking speed.
- **Location 5:** The wearer does not take any penalties for fighting underwater.
- **Location 6:** The wearer can cast *Water Walk* once per day.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of *Water Breathing* and has a +5 bonus to AC against aquatic creatures.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Popular among dwarves who live near the great underground lakes.

---

## **Visual**
A blue, wave-like pattern etched in silver that seems to flow like liquid.

---

## **The Legend of the Aquatic Adaptation**
- Legend says it was first created for the **Lake-Guardians of Naucrian**.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Master Alfrid:** A smith who spent years studying the aquatic life of the great underground lakes.
