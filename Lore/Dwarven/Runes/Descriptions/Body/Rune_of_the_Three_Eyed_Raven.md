# Rune of the Three-Eyed Raven

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Thrir** (Dwarven for 'Three')
- **Hrafn** (Dwarven for 'Raven')
> Meaning: "The Raven of Triple Sight"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Offers the wearer brief visions or insights into future events, granting bonuses to defense and initiative.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+20**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+1 insight bonus** to AC and Initiative.
- **Location 2:** Once per day, the wearer can use *Divination*.
- **Location 3:** The wearer cannot be caught flat-footed.
- **Location 4:** Insight bonus increases to **+2**.
- **Location 5:** Once per day, the wearer can reroll a failed save.
- **Location 6:** The wearer gains the *Evasion* ability while wearing the armor.
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of the *Foresight* spell.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- This rune is highly mystical and often associated with prophets and kings.

---

## **Visual**
A raven with a third eye in its forehead, glowing with a soft, ethereal violet light.

---

## **The Legend of the Three-Eyed Raven**
- Legend says it was first gifted to a dwarven seer who predicted the fall of the mountain kingdoms.
