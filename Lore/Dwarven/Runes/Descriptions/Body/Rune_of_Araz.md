# Rune of Araz

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Araz** (Dwarven interpretation of 'Life/Vitality')
> Meaning: "The Rune of Infinite Vitality"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants a supernatural boost to the wearer's constitution and overall health.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains a **+2 enhancement bonus to Constitution**.
- **Location 2:** **N/A**
- **Location 3:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on saves against poisons.
- **Location 4:** **N/A**
- **Location 5:** The enhancement bonus to Constitution increases to **+4**.
- **Location 6:** **N/A**
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The enhancement bonus to Constitution increases to **+6**.

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Does not stack with other enhancement bonuses to Constitution.

---

## **Visual**
A series of flowing, intertwining lines that represent the circulatory system, glowing with a warm pinkish-red light.

---

## **The Legend of Araz**
- Created for the **Plague-Doctors** of the Grand Forge to allow them to work among the sick without falling ill.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Healer Thalric:** A smith who studied the connection between metal and biology.
