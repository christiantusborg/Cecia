# Rune of Deepdelve

**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Djúp** (Dwarven for 'Deep')
- **Delfa** (Dwarven for 'Delve/Dig')
> Meaning: "The Deep Delver's Rune"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Grants darkvision beyond the normal dwarven range and enhances subterranean perception.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **4.1 Rune Difficulty**
**+10**

### **4.2 Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4.3 Effect Variation Based on Location**
- **Location 1:** The wearer gains **Darkvision up to 60 feet** (if they already have it, it increases by 30 feet).
- **Location 2:** The wearer gains a **+2 bonus** on Search and Listen checks while underground.
- **Location 3:** The wearer can use *Detect Secret Doors* once per day.
- **Location 4:** Darkvision increases to 120 feet.
- **Location 5:** The wearer gains the *Stonecunning* ability (if they don't already have it; if they do, the bonus increases to +4).
- **Location 6:** Once per day, the wearer can use *Clairaudience/Clairvoyance* (underground only).
- **Location 7:** [Soul Swords Only - applied to Armor] The wearer is permanently under the effect of *True Seeing* (underground only).

### **4.4 Additional or Specific Rules**
- Standard for deep-tunnel explorers and miners.

---

## **Visual**
A tunnel-like pattern glowing with a soft white-grey light.

---

## **The Legend of the Deepdelve**
- Forged for the **First Explorers** who descended into the deepest caverns.

---

## **Description of Creator**
- **Explorer Kael:** A dwarf who spent more time in the tunnels than in the cities.
