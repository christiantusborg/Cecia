# Rune of Grip of the Earth

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Grip** (Dwarven for 'Grip')
- **Jord** (Dwarven for 'Earth')
> Meaning: "Fixed-Point"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Causes furniture to remain fixed.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; Command word to fix/release.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+8** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Objects cannot be moved or knocked over.
- Effectively increases weight by 1000 lbs.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Roots spreading from the object's base.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used on ships and during quakes.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Domestic Smithy
