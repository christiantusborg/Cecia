# Rune of Sun-Sigil

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sol** (Dwarven for 'Sun')
- **Sig** (Dwarven for 'Mark')
> Meaning: "Ever-Light"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Provides steady light.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; Command word to toggle.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Sheds bright light in 20ft, dim in 40ft.
- Lasts indefinitely.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A bright yellow rune that glows.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Replaces torches in cities.
