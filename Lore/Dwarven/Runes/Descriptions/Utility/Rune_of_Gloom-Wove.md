# Rune of Gloom-Wove

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Mørk** (Dwarven for 'Dark')
- **Vev** (Dwarven for 'Weave')
> Meaning: "Shadow-Patch"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporary patch of magical darkness.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+20** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- 20ft radius of 'Darkness' (as spell).
- Duration: 1 minute.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Pulling an invisible curtain across the air.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by the Shadow-Smiths.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Night-Guard
