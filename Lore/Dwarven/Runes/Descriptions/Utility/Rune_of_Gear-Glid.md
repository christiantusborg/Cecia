# Rune of Gear-Glid

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Gir** (Dwarven for 'Gear')
- **Glid** (Dwarven for 'Slide')
> Meaning: "Smooth-Work"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Lubricates machinery.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; applies to mechanical parts.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Negates penalties from lack of oil.
- Increases efficiency of machines by 20%.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Two interlocking circles with oil drops.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used in lift systems and mills.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Engineers' Society
