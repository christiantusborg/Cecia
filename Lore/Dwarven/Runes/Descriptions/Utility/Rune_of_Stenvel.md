# Rune of Stenvel

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sten** (Dwarven for 'Stone')
- **Vel** (Dwarven for 'Power')
> Meaning: "Stone Strength"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Reveals strength of mountains.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; touching stone (Standard).

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Reveals structural weaknesses and hollows.
- +5 to Knowledge (Architecture).

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A flexed arm made of granite.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by builders and sappers.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Stone-Talkers
