# Rune of Quake-Tap

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Skjálf** (Dwarven for 'Quake/Vibration')
- **Pikk** (Dwarven for 'Tap/Touch')
> Meaning: "Vibration-Touch"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Creates a small, controlled vibration in a touched object.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air and touched to an object.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Causes a small object (up to 5 lbs) to vibrate for 1 round.
- Can be used to shake off loose dust, check for cracks in stone (providing a +2 bonus to relevant Search checks), or create a minor distraction.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A quick, repetitive tapping motion in the air ending with a finger press against the target.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Commonly used by miners to test the stability of a rock face by listening to the resonance.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Stone-Listeners
