# Rune of Breath-Draft

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Pust** (Dwarven for 'Breath')
- **Vind** (Dwarven for 'Wind')
> Meaning: "Fresh-Air-Call"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Creates a puff of fresh air.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Clears smoke/gas in a 10ft cube.
- Provides 1 minute of breathable air.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Blowing through a circled hand.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Emergency air supply.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Shaft Wardens
