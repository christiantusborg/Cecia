# Rune of Aleflow

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Al** (Dwarven for 'Ale')
- **Flo** (Dwarven for 'Stream')
> Meaning: "Ever-Full Cask"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Ensures kegs never run dry (slowly refills).

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; refills 1 gallon per hour.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+20** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Refills a cask with standard Dwarven Ale.
- Cannot be used for high-end spirits.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Foamy white swirls on the tap.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
The dream of every barkeep.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Hanseath's Chosen
