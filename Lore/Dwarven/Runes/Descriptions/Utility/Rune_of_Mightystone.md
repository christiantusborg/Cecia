# Rune of Mightystone

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Makt** (Dwarven for 'Might')
- **Sten** (Dwarven for 'Rock')
> Meaning: "Unbreakable Wall"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Increases durability of stone structures.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; reinforces material.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+20** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Double the Hit Points of the stone structure.
- Immune to 'Shatter' and 'Passwall'.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Heavy, blocky runes carved deep.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used in fortress gates.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Stonebound Guard
