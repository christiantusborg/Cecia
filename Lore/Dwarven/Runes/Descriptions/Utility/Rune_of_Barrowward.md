# Rune of Barrowward

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Bar** (Dwarven for 'Grave')
- **Ward** (Dwarven for 'Guard')
> Meaning: "Rest-Eternal"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Protective ward against undead.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; 30ft radius.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+20** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Undead must succeed on a Will DC 18 to enter.
- Grants +4 Turn Resistance to allies.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A silver skull behind bars.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Placed on tombs and burial sites.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Priests of Dumathoin
