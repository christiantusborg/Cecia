# Rune of Iron-Hard

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Jern** (Dwarven for 'Iron')
- **Hard** (Dwarven for 'Hard')
> Meaning: "Toughen-Gear"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporarily toughens equipment.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Swift Action; Traced over gear.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Increases Hardness by +5 and HP by +10.
- Duration: 10 minutes.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A heavy striking motion toward the object.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Prevents gear breakage.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Battle-Forged
