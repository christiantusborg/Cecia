# Rune of Trap-Tripper

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Gild** (Dwarven for 'Trap')
- **Snub** (Dwarven for 'Trip')
> Meaning: "Danger-Sense"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Detects mechanical traps.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; glows when trap is within 10ft.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +4 to Search and Disable Device checks.
- Allows Search at full movement speed.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A snapped spring sigil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by scouts and sappers.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Sappers' Elite
