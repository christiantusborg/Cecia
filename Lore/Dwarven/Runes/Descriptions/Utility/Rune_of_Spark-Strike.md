# Rune of Spark-Strike

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Gnist** (Dwarven for 'Spark')
- **Slag** (Dwarven for 'Strike')
> Meaning: "Fire-Starter"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Ignites flammable materials.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Move Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Ignites any flammable object within 10ft.
- Deals 1d4 fire damage to a creature.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A snapping motion with thumb and middle finger.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Replaces flint and steel.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Commoners' Friend
