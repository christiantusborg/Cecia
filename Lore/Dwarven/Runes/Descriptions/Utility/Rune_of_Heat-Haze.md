# Rune of Heat-Haze

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Hæt** (Dwarven for 'Heat')
- **Sky** (Dwarven for 'Cloud')
> Meaning: "Shimmer-Shield"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Creates minor visual distortion.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Grants 20% concealment for 3 rounds.
- Penalties to ranged attacks against user.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Waving the hand in a rippling motion.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for evasion.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Desert Scouts
