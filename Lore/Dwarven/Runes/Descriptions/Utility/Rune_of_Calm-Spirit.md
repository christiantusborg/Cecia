# Rune of Calm-Spirit

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Stil** (Dwarven for 'Quiet')
- **Sjel** (Dwarven for 'Spirit')
> Meaning: "Beast-Quiet"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Reduces fear/panic in animals.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Animal must save (Will DC 14) or lose 'Frightened' status.
- +4 to Handle Animal for 1 minute.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A slow, downward petting motion.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by mountain handlers.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Beastmasters
