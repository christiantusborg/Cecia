# Rune of Metal-Ping

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Malm** (Dwarven for 'Metal')
- **Klang** (Dwarven for 'Ping/Ring')
> Meaning: "Iron-Chime"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Causes a metal object to emit a clear, resonant tone.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air toward a metal object within 30 feet.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Target metal object (up to 10 lbs) rings clearly as if struck by a hammer.
- Can be heard up to 100 feet away.
- Useful for signalling or checking for hollows in metal structures.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A flicking motion towards the target object.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by smiths to check the purity of their work and by sentries as a simple alarm system.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Anvil-Singers
