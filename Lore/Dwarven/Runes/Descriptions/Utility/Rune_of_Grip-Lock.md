# Rune of Grip-Lock

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Grip** (Dwarven for 'Grip')
- **Fast** (Dwarven for 'Fixed')
> Meaning: "Stick-Tite"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporarily sticks objects together.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Two objects are magically fused for 1 minute.
- Strength DC 20 to separate.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Interlocking the fingers of both hands.

## **Lore & Origin**
Quick repair or trap.
