# Rune of Meadbless

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Með** (Dwarven for 'Honey-wine')
- **Bles** (Dwarven for 'Sanctify')
> Meaning: "Pure Draught"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Preserves food and drink.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects containers it is inscribed upon.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Prevents spoilage for up to 1 year.
- Removes minor toxins from liquid.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A golden drop-shaped sigil on barrels.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Sacred to the followers of Hanseath.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Brewers' Guild
