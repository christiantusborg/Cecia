# Rune of Metal-Meld

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Malm** (Dwarven for 'Metal')
- **Smelt** (Dwarven for 'Fuse')
> Meaning: "Weld-Magic"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Assists in welding/fusing metal.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; during Craft check.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +5 to Craft (Blacksmithing) for joining parts.
- Joints are stronger than the base metal.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Two bars merging into one.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Standard in high-quality smithing.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Blacksmiths' Guild
