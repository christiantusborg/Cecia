# Rune of Sentry-Eye

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vakt** (Dwarven for 'Watch')
- **Syn** (Dwarven for 'Eye')
> Meaning: "Hidden-Alarm"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Invisible alarm.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; triggers when line is crossed.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Sends mental ping to owner up to 1 mile.
- Audible alarm optional.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** An eye that is only visible to 'Detect Magic'.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Security for private homes.
