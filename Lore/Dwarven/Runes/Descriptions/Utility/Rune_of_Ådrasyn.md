# Rune of Ådrasyn

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ådra** (Dwarven for 'Vein')
- **Syn** (Dwarven for 'Vision')
> Meaning: "Sight of Gold"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Illuminates hidden wealth.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; requires 1 round of focus.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+18** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- See through 5ft of rock to find gems.
- Detects magical ores within 30ft.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** An eye with a diamond pupil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
The master's tool.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Goldsmiths' Elite
