# Rune of Stoneclad

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sten** (Dwarven for 'Stone')
- **Klæd** (Dwarven for 'Armor')
> Meaning: "Mountain's Skin"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Reinforces the structure of a building.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; always active.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Increases Hardness of stone by +5.
- Grants +10 to saves against Earthquakes.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Interlocking grey lines along the foundations.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Developed after the Great Collapse.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Deepdelve Architects
