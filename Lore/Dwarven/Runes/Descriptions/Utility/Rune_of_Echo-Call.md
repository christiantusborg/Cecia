# Rune of Echo-Call

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ekko** (Dwarven for 'Echo')
- **Kal** (Dwarven for 'Call')
> Meaning: "Forge-Phone"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Communication between distant points.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; touching the rune (Standard).

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Two-way voice communication between linked runes.
- Range: 5 miles.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A mouth and an ear connected.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for coordination in large forges.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Signal Corps
