# Rune of Caskseal

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Kask** (Dwarven for 'Cask')
- **Sel** (Dwarven for 'Seal')
> Meaning: "Tamper-Proof"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Magically seals barrels.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; Command word to lock/unlock.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- DC 25 Strength check to break seal.
- Changes color if liquid is poisoned.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A purple wax-like sigil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by merchants for shipping.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Trade Commission
