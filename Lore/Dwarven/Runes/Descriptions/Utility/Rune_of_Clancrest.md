# Rune of Clancrest

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Klan** (Dwarven for 'Family')
- **Krest** (Dwarven for 'Seal')
> Meaning: "Ancestral Mark"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Marks property and wards off thieves.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; visual and alarm.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Alarm triggers if a non-clan member touches the object.
- +5 to Intimidate against intruders.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** The specific heraldry of a clan, glowing faintly.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
The basic mark of ownership.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
High Kings' Law
