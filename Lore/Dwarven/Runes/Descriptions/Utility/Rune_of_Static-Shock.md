# Rune of Static-Shock

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Gnist** (Dwarven for 'Spark')
- **Lost** (Dwarven for 'Shock/Strike')
> Meaning: "Spark-Sting"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Discharges a small but painful spark of electricity.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air and touched to a target.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Deals 1d3 electricity damage on a successful melee touch attack.
- The user gains a +2 bonus to the attack roll if the target is wearing metal armor.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A jagged, zig-zag motion with the index finger.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by pranksters and as a minor deterrent against unwanted contact.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Storm-Callers (Junior)
