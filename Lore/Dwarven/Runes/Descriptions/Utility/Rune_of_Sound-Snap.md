# Rune of Sound-Snap

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Lyd** (Dwarven for 'Sound')
- **Smel** (Dwarven for 'Crack')
> Meaning: "Noise-Maker"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Creates a loud distracting noise.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Creates noise as loud as a thunderclap at target point.
- Will save DC 14 to avoid being distracted.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Clapping hands together sharply.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for diversions.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Infiltrators
