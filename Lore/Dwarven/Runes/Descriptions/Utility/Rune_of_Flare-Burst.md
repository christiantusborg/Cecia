# Rune of Flare-Burst

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Glim** (Dwarven for 'Flash')
- **Bryst** (Dwarven for 'Burst')
> Meaning: "Blinding-Light"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Bright light to blind.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Targets within 15ft must save (Fort DC 14) or be blinded 1 round.
- Everyone is dazzled for 1 minute.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Closing and suddenly opening the fist.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for signaling and distraction.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Illuminators
