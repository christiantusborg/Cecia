# Rune of Stone-Shatter

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sten** (Dwarven for 'Stone')
- **Brost** (Dwarven for 'Break')
> Meaning: "Rock-Cracker"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Breaks small stones/locks.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Deals 3d6 damage to non-living stone/metal.
- Ignores Hardness 10.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A sharp tapping motion against the air.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
The miner's quick tool.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Sappers
