# Rune of Glimmerward

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Glim** (Dwarven for 'Shine')
- **Ward** (Dwarven for 'Protect')
> Meaning: "Treasure Glow"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Makes metals/gems shine brighter.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; cosmetic.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +2 to Appraise checks in the room.
- Increases light level by one step.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Sparkling gold dust integrated into the rune.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used in vaults to show off wealth.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Gilders' Society
