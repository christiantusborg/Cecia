# Rune of Ironhoof

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Jern** (Dwarven for 'Iron')
- **Hof** (Dwarven for 'Hoof')
> Meaning: "Sure-Foot"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Hardens mount hooves.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; active while riding.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Mount ignores difficult terrain from rubble.
- +2 to mount's AC.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Metallic grey circles on the hooves.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by heavy cavalry.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Ram-Riders
