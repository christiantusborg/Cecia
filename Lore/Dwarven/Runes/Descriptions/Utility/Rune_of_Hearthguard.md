# Rune of Hearthguard

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Harth** (Dwarven for 'Hearth')
- **Garth** (Dwarven for 'Guard')
> Meaning: "Home Shield"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Protects a dwelling from fire and extreme heat.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; triggers when heat exceeds 150°F.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Provides Fire Resistance 10 to the structure.
- Douses non-magical fires automatically.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** An amber-glowing circle placed above the fireplace.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Common in every Dwarven home.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
House of Ironbeard
