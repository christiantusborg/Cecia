# Rune of Windbarrier

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vind** (Dwarven for 'Wind')
- **Bar** (Dwarven for 'Block')
> Meaning: "Storm Stopper"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Protects from harsh winds.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; creates a 20ft dome of calm.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Penalties to ranged attacks from wind are negated.
- Structures take no damage from gale-force winds.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Swirling blue patterns on exterior walls.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used in high mountain outposts.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Skywatcher Tradition
