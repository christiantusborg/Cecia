# Rune of Vermin-Bane

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Orm** (Dwarven for 'Vermin')
- **Bann** (Dwarven for 'Banish')
> Meaning: "Rat-Rid"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Keeps pests away.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; 40ft radius.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Insects, rats, and other vermin will not enter.
- +4 to saves against disease in the area.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A crossed-out spider sigil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Essential for granaries.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Farmers' Guild
