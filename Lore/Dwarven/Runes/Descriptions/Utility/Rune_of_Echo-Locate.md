# Rune of Echo-Locate

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ekko** (Dwarven for 'Echo')
- **Finn** (Dwarven for 'Find')
> Meaning: "Sound-Map"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Mapping nearby tunnels.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Reveals layout of tunnels within 120ft.
- Identifies secret passages (Search DC 20).
