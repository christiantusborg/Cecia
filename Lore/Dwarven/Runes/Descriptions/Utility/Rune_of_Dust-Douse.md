# Rune of Dust-Douse

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Støv** (Dwarven for 'Dust')
- **Vat** (Dwarven for 'Wet')
> Meaning: "Air-Cleaner"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Settles coal dust in the air.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; 20ft area.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Dust particles are pulled to the floor.
- +2 to saves against lung disease.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Falling dots merging with the ground.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Standard in coal mines.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Health & Safety Bureau
