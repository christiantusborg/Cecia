# Rune of Hearth-Warmth

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Harth** (Dwarven for 'Hearth')
- **Varm** (Dwarven for 'Warmth')
> Meaning: "Winter-Shield"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Maintains comfortable temperature.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; always active.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Temperature stays at 72°F in 30ft radius.
- Negates 'Chill Touch' environmental effects.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A sun-like circle with 8 rays.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Standard for surface-dwelling dwarves.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Domestic Smithy
