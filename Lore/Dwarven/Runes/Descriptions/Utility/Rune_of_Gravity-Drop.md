# Rune of Gravity-Drop

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Þryng** (Dwarven for 'Pressure/Force')
- **Fall** (Dwarven for 'Fall/Drop')
> Meaning: "Heavy-Drop"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Briefly triples the weight of an object, causing it to fall or become hard to hold.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air targeting an object within 20 feet.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Triples the weight of an object (max 50 lbs base weight) for 1 round.
- If held, the holder must make a DC 15 Strength check to keep from dropping it or being pulled down.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A heavy downward sweep of the hand.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Originally used to secure heavy loads that were slipping, now also used for minor sabotage.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Deep-Haulers
