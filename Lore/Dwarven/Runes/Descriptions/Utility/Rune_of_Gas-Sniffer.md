# Rune of Gas-Sniffer

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Gas** (Dwarven for 'Vapor')
- **Snik** (Dwarven for 'Detect')
> Meaning: "Foul-Air Finder"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Detects toxic gases.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; glows red when gas is detected.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+8** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Detects 'Cloudkill', 'Stinking Cloud', and natural toxins.
- Works up to 60ft away.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A nose sigil that flares orange.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Safety standard for all miners.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Deepdelve Safety Board
