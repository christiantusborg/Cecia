# Rune of Weight-Lift

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Þryng** (Dwarven for 'Weight/Pressure')
- **Haf** (Dwarven for 'Lift')
> Meaning: "Light-Lift"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Briefly reduces the effective weight of an object.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air targeting an object within 10 feet.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Halves the effective weight of an object (max 100 lbs base weight) for 1 round.
- Does not affect the object's mass or durability, only how it interacts with gravity.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** An upward lifting motion with both palms facing the sky.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by laborers to move heavy stones or crates that would otherwise require multiple dwarves.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Deep-Haulers
