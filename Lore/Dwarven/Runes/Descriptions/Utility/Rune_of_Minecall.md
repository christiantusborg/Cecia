# Rune of Minecall

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Min** (Dwarven for 'Mine')
- **Kal** (Dwarven for 'Seek')
> Meaning: "Ore-Whistle"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Leads miners to nearest veins.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; triggers when hammered (Standard).

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Points toward the largest ore deposit within 100ft.
- Glows brighter as you get closer.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A compass needle made of pickaxes.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Essential for new excavations.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Prospectors' Union
