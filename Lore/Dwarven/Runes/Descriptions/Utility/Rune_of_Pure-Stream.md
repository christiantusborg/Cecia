# Rune of Pure-Stream

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Varr** (Dwarven for 'Water')
- **Ren** (Dwarven for 'Clean')
> Meaning: "Clear-Flow"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Purifies water.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects water passing over it.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Removes all non-magical disease and poison.
- Improves taste significantly.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A crystal-clear drop sigil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Found on all city wells.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
City Water Works
