# Rune of Echo-Ping

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Bergmál** (Dwarven for 'Echo')
- **Pikk** (Dwarven for 'Ping/Tap')
> Meaning: "Sound-Search"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Emits a sonar-like pulse that helps reveal the surroundings.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Grants a +2 competence bonus on Listen checks for 1 minute.
- Can detect hollow spaces or large objects within 10 feet, even through stone.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** An expanding circle traced in the air that emits a faint hum.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
An essential tool for scouts and miners navigating through unknown or collapsed tunnels.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Echo-Walkers
