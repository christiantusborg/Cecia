# Rune of Light-Snap

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ljós** (Dwarven for 'Light')
- **Smell** (Dwarven for 'Snap/Crack')
> Meaning: "Sudden-Light"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Produces a brief, bright flash of light.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+8** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Produces a flash that illuminates a 20-foot radius for 1 round.
- Any creature within 5 feet of the flash must succeed on a DC 12 Fortitude save or be dazzled for 1 round.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A sharp snapping motion of the fingers after tracing a circular sigil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Developed for dark tunnels where a temporary light is needed without the permanence of a torch.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Tunnel-Guides
