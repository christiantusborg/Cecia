# Rune of Stablegrace

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Stab** (Dwarven for 'Pen')
- **Gras** (Dwarven for 'Calm')
> Meaning: "Beast Peace"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Calms livestock.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; 30ft aura.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+8** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Animals are never spooked by natural sounds.
- +4 to Handle Animal checks.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A peaceful animal head sigil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Essential for underground ranches.

## **Creator/Tradition**
