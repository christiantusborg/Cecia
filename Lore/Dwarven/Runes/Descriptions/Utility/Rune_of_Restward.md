# Rune of Restward

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Rest** (Dwarven for 'Sleep')
- **Ward** (Dwarven for 'Guard')
> Meaning: "Dream Shield"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Ensures peaceful and rejuvenating rest.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects those sleeping within 10ft.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Heals 1 extra HP per level during rest.
- Immune to 'Nightmare' spell effects.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A crescent moon resting on a stone pillow.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Found in guest houses and barracks.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Healers of Berronar
