# Rune of Veinreader

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Væn** (Dwarven for 'Vein')
- **Ræd** (Dwarven for 'Discover')
> Meaning: "Ore Seer"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Indicates nearby valuable minerals.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; requires touching the rune (Standard Action).

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Grants +5 to Knowledge (Dungeoneering) to find ore.
- Vibrates when within 10ft of gold or mithril.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Thin silver lines that pulse like a heartbeat.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by master prospectors.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Prospectors' Union
