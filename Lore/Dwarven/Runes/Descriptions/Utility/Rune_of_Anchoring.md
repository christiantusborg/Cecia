# Rune of Anchoring

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ank** (Dwarven for 'Anchor')
- **Hold** (Dwarven for 'Stay')
> Meaning: "Will-Link"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Prevents forced teleportation.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; triggers on teleport attempt.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+22** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Wielder can choose to automatically fail saves against 'Teleport' or 'Banishment'.
- Prevents 'Blink' or 'Ethereal Jaunt' unless desired.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** An iron chain wrapping the item.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used to prevent kidnapping.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Royal Guard
