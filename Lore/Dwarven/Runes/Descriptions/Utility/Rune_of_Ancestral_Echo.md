# Rune of Ancestral Echo

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ank** (Dwarven for 'Ancient')
- **Ekko** (Dwarven for 'Resonance')
> Meaning: "Voice of the Past"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Allows dwelling to resonate with past voices.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; requires 1 minute of meditation.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+25** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Can ask one question of the 'house' per day (as Commune).
- Gives advice on local history.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Ghostly white runes that shimmer.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Found in ancient clan halls.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Lorekeepers of Dugmaren
