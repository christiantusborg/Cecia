# Rune of Water-Whip

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vatn** (Dwarven for 'Water')
- **Svipa** (Dwarven for 'Whip')
> Meaning: "Lashing-Water"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Condenses moisture from the air into a brief, stinging lash of water.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Creates a lash of water that can strike a target up to 10 feet away.
- Deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage on a successful ranged touch attack.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A cracking whip motion with the hand.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used to clear debris from a distance or as a non-lethal deterrent against small pests.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Well-Springers
