# Rune of Blood-Key

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Blod** (Dwarven for 'Blood')
- **Nøk** (Dwarven for 'Key')
> Meaning: "Lineage-Lock"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Only allows specific people/lineages.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; identity check.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+25** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Only opens/activates for specific bloodline.
- Deals 3d6 damage to unauthorized users.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A red vein pattern in the rune.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for royal archives.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Royal Lineage Guard
