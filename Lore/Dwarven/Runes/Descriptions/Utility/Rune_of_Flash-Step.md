# Rune of Flash-Step

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Snar** (Dwarven for 'Swift')
- **Steg** (Dwarven for 'Step')
> Meaning: "Blink-Dash"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Short range teleport/dash.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Teleport up to 30ft in line of sight.
- Does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A quick horizontal slash followed by a vertical flick.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by elite vanguard.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Storm-Runners
