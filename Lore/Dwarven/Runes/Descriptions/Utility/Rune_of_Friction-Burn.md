# Rune of Friction-Burn

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Nudd** (Dwarven for 'Friction/Rub')
- **Svíða** (Dwarven for 'Burn/Sting')
> Meaning: "Grip-Sting"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Increases the friction of an object's surface intensely for a brief moment.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air toward an object within 15 feet.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- The target object becomes incredibly hot and sticky due to increased friction for 1 round.
- If the object is being held, disarming the wielder becomes more difficult (+2 DC to any disarm attempt against them), but the wielder takes 1 point of fire damage from the heat.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A vigorous rubbing motion of the palms followed by a point.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used to ensure a firm grip on tools during strenuous labor, or to make an enemy's weapon painful to hold.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Grip-Masters
