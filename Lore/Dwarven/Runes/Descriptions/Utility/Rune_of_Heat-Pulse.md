# Rune of Heat-Pulse

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Hita** (Dwarven for 'Heat')
- **Bylgja** (Dwarven for 'Pulse/Wave')
> Meaning: "Warmth-Wave"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Sends a brief wave of intense heat toward a touched point.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air and touched to a target.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Deals 1d4 fire damage to a touched creature or object.
- Instantly melts up to 1 cubic foot of ice or snow.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A palm-out pushing motion that radiates a red glow.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Commonly used to thaw frozen locks or provide a quick spark for a fire.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Forge-Tenders
