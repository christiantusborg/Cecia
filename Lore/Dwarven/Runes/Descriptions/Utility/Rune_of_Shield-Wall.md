# Rune of Shield-Wall

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Skjold** (Dwarven for 'Shield')
- **Veg** (Dwarven for 'Wall')
> Meaning: "Force-Barrier"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporary force barrier.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Immediate Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+18** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Grants +4 Shield bonus to AC for 1 round.
- Absorbs one 'Magic Missile' volley.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A wide semi-circular sweep with a flat palm.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Basic defense for rune-mages.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Guard-Wards
