# Rune of Haste-Hand

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Flit** (Dwarven for 'Fast')
- **Hand** (Dwarven for 'Hand')
> Meaning: "Speed-Tool"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporary speed boost for tasks.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Swift Action; Traced over hands.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Double speed for manual Craft checks for 1 hour.
- +2 to Initiative for 1 round.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A rapid vibrating movement of the fingers.

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by master smiths.
