# Rune of Deepharmony

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Djup** (Dwarven for 'Deep')
- **Harm** (Dwarven for 'Tune')
> Meaning: "Stone Song"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Enhances acoustics in underground homes.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; auditory enhancement.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +4 to Perform (Sing/Oratory) checks.
- Sound carries clearly without echoing.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Musical notes shaped like stalactites.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Found in dwarven opera houses.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Choral Society of Moradin
