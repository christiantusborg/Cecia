# Rune of Tread-Sure

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Træ** (Dwarven for 'Step')
- **Vis** (Dwarven for 'Sure')
> Meaning: "Non-Slip"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Prevents slipping on wet surfaces.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; 30ft area.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+8** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Balance and Tumble checks have DC -5.
- Immune to 'Grease' spell effects in area.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A boot with deep treads.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used on metal walkways.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Maintenance Crews
