# Rune of Flame-Flick

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Eld** (Dwarven for 'Flame/Fire')
- **Blik** (Dwarven for 'Flicker/Gleam')
> Meaning: "Small-Fire"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Produces a tiny, momentary flame at the user's fingertip.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Instantly lights a torch, candle, or other easily flammable material.
- If used as an attack, it deals 1 point of fire damage on a successful touch.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A quick snapping motion that leaves a trail of sparks.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
The most basic utility rune, taught to every apprentice for everyday lighting needs.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Hearth-Keepers
