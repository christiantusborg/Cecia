# Rune of Ingwaz

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ing** (Dwarven for 'Nature')
- **Waz** (Dwarven for 'Growth')
> Meaning: "Seed of Growth"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Enhances crop yields and plant health.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects area within 30ft.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +4 bonus to Profession (Farmer) and Survival checks in the area.
- Plants grow 50% faster.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A lozenge-shaped sigil carved into wooden posts or boundary stones.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Essential for mountain farming.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Vigrid Clan
