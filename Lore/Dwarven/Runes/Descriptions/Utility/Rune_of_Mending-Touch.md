# Rune of Mending-Touch

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Bot** (Dwarven for 'Repair')
- **Traf** (Dwarven for 'Touch')
> Meaning: "Fix-It"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Repairs minor damage to tools.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced over object.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+8** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Repairs 1d8+CL hit points to an object.
- Can fuse a broken tool back together.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Tracing the outline of the break.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Handy for all craftsmen.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Artisans' Guild
