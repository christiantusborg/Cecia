# Rune of Ridgeback

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Rig** (Dwarven for 'Spine')
- **Bak** (Dwarven for 'Support')
> Meaning: "Heavy-Lift Mount"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Strengthens mount's back.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; while equipped.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Increases mount's carrying capacity by 50%.
- Mount ignores fatigue from heavy loads.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A vertical line with horizontal ribs.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by mountain traders.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Caravan Masters
