# Rune of Ward of Welcoming

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vel** (Dwarven for 'Well')
- **Kom** (Dwarven for 'Come')
> Meaning: "Guest-Grace"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Ensures guests feel welcomed.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; 20ft aura.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +2 to Diplomacy and Gather Information.
- Calms aggressive emotions (Will DC 14).

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** An open door sigil in bright green.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Placed on guesthouse doors.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Hospitality House
