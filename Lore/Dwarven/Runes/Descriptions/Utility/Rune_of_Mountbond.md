# Rune of Mountbond

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Munt** (Dwarven for 'Mount')
- **Band** (Dwarven for 'Link')
> Meaning: "Rider's Unity"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Strengthens bond with a mount.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; active while riding.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +4 bonus to Ride checks.
- Mount gains +2 to Will saves.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A horseshoe entwined with a braid.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by the Ram-Riders of Lagon.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Beastmasters' Circle
