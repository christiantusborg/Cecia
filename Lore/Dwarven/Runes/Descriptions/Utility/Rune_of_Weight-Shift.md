# Rune of Weight-Shift

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vekt** (Dwarven for 'Weight')
- **Byt** (Dwarven for 'Change')
> Meaning: "Gravity-Tweak"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporarily alters gravity on an object.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+20** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Target object becomes weightless or 10x heavier.
- Duration: 5 rounds.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Moving hands together then apart vertically.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for logistics.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Engineers' Society
