# Rune of Air-Pocket

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vind** (Dwarven for 'Air/Wind')
- **Hylki** (Dwarven for 'Pocket/Capsule')
> Meaning: "Breath-Bubble"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Creates a small pocket of fresh, breathable air.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air around the user's face.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Creates a 1-foot diameter bubble of fresh air that lasts for 1 minute.
- Provides immunity to inhaled toxins and drowning for the duration while the user remains within the pocket.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A cupping motion with both hands.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
A life-saving rune for dwarves trapped in gas-filled tunnels or underwater.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Shaft-Breathers
