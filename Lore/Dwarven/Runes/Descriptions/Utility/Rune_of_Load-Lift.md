# Rune of Load-Lift

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Lad** (Dwarven for 'Load')
- **Lyft** (Dwarven for 'Rise')
> Meaning: "Weight-Lightener"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Reduces weight of heavy loads.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; Command word to activate.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Reduces weight of target object by 75%.
- Lasts 8 hours.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** An arrow pointing upward.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by porters and builders.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Laborers' Union
