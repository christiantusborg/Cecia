# Rune of Insight-Gleam

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Væt** (Dwarven for 'Wit')
- **Syn** (Dwarven for 'Vision')
> Meaning: "Truth-Sight"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Reveals hidden objects.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+18** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Grants 'See Invisibility' for 1 minute.
- +5 to Search for 1 round.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Circling the eye with a finger.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used to find secret doors.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Seekers of Knowledge
