# Rune of Stone-Soothe

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sten** (Dwarven for 'Stone')
- **Lína** (Dwarven for 'Soothe/Smooth')
> Meaning: "Rock-Smoother"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Temporarily softens the surface of stone to allow for easy smoothing or shaping.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air and applied to a stone surface.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+8** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Grants a +2 competence bonus on a Craft (stonemasonry) or relevant check to shape stone.
- Can be used to remove rough edges from a 1-foot square area of stone instantly.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A gentle, circular stroking motion in the air.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by master masons to put the finishing touches on delicate stonework.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Master-Builders
