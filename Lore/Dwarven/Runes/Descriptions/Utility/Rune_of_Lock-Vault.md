# Rune of Lock-Vault

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Lok** (Dwarven for 'Lock')
- **Vat** (Dwarven for 'Strong')
> Meaning: "Unbreakable-Seal"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Magically seals locks.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; Command word to lock.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+20** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Open Lock DC increases by +15.
- Immune to 'Knock' (CL < 15).

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A keyhole filled with silver liquid.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Found on bank vaults.
