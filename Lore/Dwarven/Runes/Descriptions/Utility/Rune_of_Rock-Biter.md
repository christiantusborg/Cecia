# Rune of Rock-Biter

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sten** (Dwarven for 'Stone')
- **Bit** (Dwarven for 'Bite')
> Meaning: "Stone-Softener"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Softens rock for easier excavation.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; touching rock (Standard).

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Temporarily reduces Hardness of stone by 10 for 1 hour.
- Makes rock easy to pick.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Jagged teeth patterns.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for rapid tunneling.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Sappers' Elite
