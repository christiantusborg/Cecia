# Rune of Salt-Savor

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Salt** (Dwarven for 'Salt')
- **Smak** (Dwarven for 'Taste')
> Meaning: "Cook's-Friend"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Seasons and preserves meat.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects food in contact.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Food is perfectly seasoned.
- Preservation lasts twice as long.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** White crystals etched in stone.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Common in mess halls.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Cooks' Guild
