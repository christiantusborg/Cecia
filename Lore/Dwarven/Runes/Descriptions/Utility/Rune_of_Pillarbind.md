# Rune of Pillarbind

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Pil** (Dwarven for 'Support')
- **Bind** (Dwarven for 'Tie')
> Meaning: "Column-Core"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Prevents collapse of supports.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; reinforces columns.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+20** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Column cannot be broken unless structure is destroyed.
- Supports 5x normal weight.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Vertical braids wrapping the pillar.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Standard in deep-mine architecture.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Deep Mine Engineers
