# Rune of Shaftguard

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Skaft** (Dwarven for 'Shaft')
- **Garth** (Dwarven for 'Guard')
> Meaning: "Cave-In Stopper"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Prevents localized cave-ins.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; stabilizes ceiling.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+18** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Ceiling cannot collapse unless structural pillars are gone.
- Prevents 'Falling Blocks' traps.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Interlocking arches in dark iron.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used in primary transit tunnels.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Tunnel Wardens
