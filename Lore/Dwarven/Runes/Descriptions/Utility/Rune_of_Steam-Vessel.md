# Rune of Steam-Vessel

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Damp** (Dwarven for 'Steam')
- **Kar** (Dwarven for 'Vessel')
> Meaning: "Pressure-Control"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Regulates steam pressure.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; prevents explosions.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Automatically vents excess pressure.
- +5 to checks to operate steam engines.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Whistling steam vents in copper.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Crucial for industrial sector.

## **Creator/Tradition**
