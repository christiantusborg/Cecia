# Rune of Scent-Trace

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Luk** (Dwarven for 'Smell')
- **Spor** (Dwarven for 'Trace')
> Meaning: "Bloodhound-Sigil"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Follow a specific smell.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Grants 'Scent' ability for 10 minutes.
- +5 to Track using scent.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** Inhaling deeply while tracing a line to the nose.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by hunters.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Trapper Clan
