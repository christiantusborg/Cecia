# Rune of Cold-Snap

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Kald** (Dwarven for 'Cold')
- **Smell** (Dwarven for 'Snap')
> Meaning: "Frost-Snap"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Creates a sudden, localized drop in temperature.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air and touched to a target.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Deals 1d4 cold damage to a touched creature or object.
- Instantly freezes up to 1 gallon of water.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A pinching motion that leaves a trail of frost in the air.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used to quickly cool hot metal or preserve small amounts of food.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Ice-Wardens
