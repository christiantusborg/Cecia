# Rune of Silentstone

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Sil** (Dwarven for 'Quiet')
- **Sten** (Dwarven for 'Stone')
> Meaning: "Hush-Rock"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Soundproofs a room.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; sound cannot cross the rune line.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Listen checks made through the wall have DC +20.
- Silences all sound within a 10ft radius.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A mouth with a finger over it, in grey.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for secret council rooms.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Whisper-Smiths
