# Rune of Forgebond

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Forg** (Dwarven for 'Tools')
- **Band** (Dwarven for 'Maintain')
> Meaning: "Rust-Bane"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Keeps tools sharp and rust-free.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects items in a rack.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Tools never degrade from age/rust.
- +1 to Craft checks using these tools.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A hammer and tongs crossed.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Standard in any professional workshop.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Grand Forge Acolytes
