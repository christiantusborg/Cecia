# Rune of Frost-Finger

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Kald** (Dwarven for 'Cold')
- **Fing** (Dwarven for 'Finger')
> Meaning: "Chill-Touch"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Freezes a small surface.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Coats 5ft area in slippery ice.
- Freezes up to 1 gallon of water.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A slow tracing with a single finger.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for preservation and traps.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Ice-Smiths
