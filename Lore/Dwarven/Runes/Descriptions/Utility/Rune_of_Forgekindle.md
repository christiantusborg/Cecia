# Rune of Forgekindle

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Forg** (Dwarven for 'Forge')
- **Kind** (Dwarven for 'Ignite')
> Meaning: "Eternal Flame"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Maintains optimal forge temperature.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; keeps heat steady.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +2 bonus to Craft (Armorsmithing/Weaponsmithing).
- Reduces fuel consumption by 50%.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A roaring flame sigil etched in obsidian.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
The pride of the Grand Forge.
