# Rune of Jordlät

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Jord** (Dwarven for 'Earth')
- **Lät** (Dwarven for 'Ease')
> Meaning: "Soil-Soft"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Earth yields its treasures easily.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects 10ft radius.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Excavation speed is doubled.
- Tools take half damage from hard rock.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A shovel slicing through soft clay.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Standard in digging teams.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Miners' Labor
