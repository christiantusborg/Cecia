# Rune of Boulderbind

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Bold** (Dwarven for 'Rock')
- **Bind** (Dwarven for 'Hold')
> Meaning: "Slide-Stop"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Stabilizes cliffs and boulders.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; affects 100ft area.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+18** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Prevents natural rockslides.
- +10 to saves against 'Transmute Rock to Mud'.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Chains made of stone patterns.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Essential for mountain safety.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Waykeepers
