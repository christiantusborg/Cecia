# Rune of Djupande

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Djup** (Dwarven for 'Deep')
- **And** (Dwarven for 'Breath')
> Meaning: "Pure Air"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Evokes fresh air deep within mines.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; cleanses 30ft radius.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Converts stale air into fresh oxygen.
- Filters smoke and light toxins.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Cloud-like wisps in pale blue.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Life-saver in deep shafts.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Deepdelve Safety Board
