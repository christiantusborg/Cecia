# Rune of Rust-Blast

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Ryð** (Dwarven for 'Rust')
- **Gusa** (Dwarven for 'Blast/Splash')
> Meaning: "Corrosion-Burst"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Causes a sudden burst of corrosive energy that affects non-magical metal.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Air Runes:** Standard Action; Traced in air targeting a metal object within 10 feet.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+15** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Deals 1d6 damage to a non-magical metal object, ignoring its Hardness.
- If used on a lock or hinge, it provides a +4 bonus on checks to break it.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Motion:** A violent flinging motion that sends a brownish-orange mist toward the target.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Originally a demolition tool, it is now used sparingly to clear old iron gates or stuck mechanisms.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Rust-Eaters
