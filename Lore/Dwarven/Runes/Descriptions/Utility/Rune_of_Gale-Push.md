# Rune of Gale-Push

**Type:** Instantaneous Air Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Vind** (Dwarven for 'Wind')
- **Skyv** (Dwarven for 'Push')
> Meaning: "Wind-Blast"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Knockback wind.

---

**+15** (Base DC for tracing)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Uncommon**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**

- +6 Strength bonus for the check.
