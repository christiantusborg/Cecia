# Rune of Mantle of the Mountain

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Mant** (Dwarven for 'Cloak')
- **Berg** (Dwarven for 'Peak')
> Meaning: "Steadfast Hold"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Increases structural integrity.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; always active.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+25** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Very Rare**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Structure is treated as an extension of the bedrock.
- Immune to 'Disintegrate'.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A mountain silhouette in white.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used for the High King's Palace.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Royal Architects
