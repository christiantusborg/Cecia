# Rune of Water-Well

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Varr** (Dwarven for 'Water')
- **Væl** (Dwarven for 'Source')
> Meaning: "Drought-Bane"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Detects underground water.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Active; vibrates near water (Move).

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+10** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Locates water up to 50ft deep.
- +5 to Survival for finding water.

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** Wavy lines that turn blue near water.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Used by frontier scouts.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Waykeepers
