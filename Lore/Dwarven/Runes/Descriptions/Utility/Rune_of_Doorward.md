# Rune of Doorward

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Dur** (Dwarven for 'Door')
- **Ward** (Dwarven for 'Lock')
> Meaning: "Gate-Shield"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Strengthens doors.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; always active.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+12** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- +10 to DC to break the door.
- Door cannot be opened by 'Knock' (CL < 10).

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A vertical bar across a circle.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Found on granaries and vaults.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Locksmiths' Guild
