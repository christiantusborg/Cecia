# Rune of Flamefellow

**Type:** Permanent Surface Rune
**Combining (Name Etymology):**
- **Flam** (Dwarven for 'Fire')
- **Fel** (Dwarven for 'Constant')
> Meaning: "Tending Flame"

---

## **Short Description or Core Effect**
Keeps hearths lit without tending.

---

## **Game Rules**

### **1. Activation & Usage**
- **Surface Runes:** Passive; consumes 1/10th normal fuel.

### **2. Rune Difficulty**
**+5** (Base DC for inscription)

### **3. Rune Rarity**
**Common**

### **4. Mechanical Effects**
- Fire never goes out unless magically doused.
- Provides steady warmth (70°F).

---

## **Visual & Tracing**
- **Appearance:** A warm orange hearth sigil.

---

## **Lore & Origin**
Found in every commoner's house.

---

## **Creator/Tradition**
Fire-Tenders Guild
