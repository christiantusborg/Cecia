### How to Use

* **Plug-and-play payroll:** Feed these figures straight into domain-turn spreadsheets or ledgers.
* **Scale locally:** ±20 % for especially rich or poor regions, or rare skills.
* **Combine duties:** Small courts can fold multiple roles into one person (e.g., Treasurer + Chamberlain).

With the added financial, judicial, provincial, and household-guard posts, this roster now covers **all key offices** a medieval-fantasy **count or duke** could reasonably employ.
