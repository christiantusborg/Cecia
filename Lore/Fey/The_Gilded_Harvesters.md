# Secret Society: The Gilded Harvesters (The Echo-Drinkers)

The **Gilded Harvesters** are a clandestine circle of Pixies that operate in the shadows of the Unseelie and Seelie courts alike. Even among the Fey, who are known for their mercurial and often dangerous nature, the Harvesters are considered **EXTREMELY dangerous** and are spoken of only in hushed whispers.

## The Philosophy: The Harvest of Souls
While most Pixies are content with play, pranks, or the stewardship of nature, the Gilded Harvesters believe that the true currency of existence is **Experience**. They do not just watch mortals; they "reap" them.

### The Practice: Memory Siphoning
The Harvesters use artifacts—often "leaky" ones like the *Soul-Anchor Amulet*—to act as automated conduits. These items are scattered across the Material Plane, passing from one desperate mortal to the next.
*   **The Siphon:** When a soul "decays" or expires within one of their artifacts, it is not destroyed. Instead, the memories, skills, and essence of that soul are compressed and funneled back to the Harvester who owns the item's "tether."
*   **The Consumption:** The Harvesters "drink" these filtered memories. This grants them thousands of years of human knowledge, the tactical brilliance of fallen generals, and the intimate secrets of kings.

## The Cost: The Madness of Many Lives
The "Harvest" comes with a horrific price for the Pixies themselves.
*   **The Influx:** To hold the memories of thousands of mortals is to lose one's own self. Many Gilded Harvesters eventually go mad, unable to distinguish their own identity from the cacophony of voices in their heads.
*   **The Bitter Grin:** This madness often manifests as a permanent, twitching smile or a complete detachment from reality, leading to their common nickname: "The Bitter-Grins."

## The Danger: Memory Wipers
The Harvesters are protective of their secrets. They are known for their aggressive use of the Pixie **Memory Loss** ability.
*   **Proactive Wiping:** If a mortal (or another Pixie) learns too much about their operations, the Harvesters will not just kill them—they will "hollow" them out, wiping their entire identity to ensure the secret of the Harvest remains hidden.
*   **The Knowledge Hunt:** They are ready to wipe the minds of entire villages if it means acquiring a specific piece of lost lore or a unique memory they desire for their collection.

## Notable Members
*   **Sylas of the Bitter-Grin:** One of the oldest and most successful Harvesters. He is rarely seen, but his artifacts—most notably the *Soul-Anchor Amulet*—have been circulating through the world for centuries, feeding him a constant stream of "refined" souls.
*   **The Eternal Pixie Queen:** Once the supreme sovereign of the Gilded Harvesters. She was the one who initially codified the laws of the Harvest and refined the "memory-drinking" rituals. However, her hunger for knowledge eventually led her to target high-level Elven secrets. In the **Year 9897 EE**, she was hunted down and **imprisoned by Anlyth of Daefaren**. Her current location is one of the most closely guarded secrets of the Ilaerothil Ath Faer, as her knowledge could either save or shatter the continent's magical foundations.
