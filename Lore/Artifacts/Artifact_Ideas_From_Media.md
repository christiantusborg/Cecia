# 100 Artifact Ideas Inspired by Movies, TV, and Books
*Designed for the D&D 3.5 ruleset in the world of Cecia. Adheres to the Law of Equilibrium: Every boon has a burden.*

## The Vampire Diaries & The Originals
1. **Lapis of the Daywalker**
   - **Power:** Grants an undead creature complete immunity to sunlight and light-based penalties.
   - **Price:** The wielder gains vulnerability (takes +50% damage) to fire and wooden piercing weapons.
2. **Signet of the Mortal Veil (Gilbert Ring)**
   - **Power:** If slain by a supernatural creature (Undead, Fey, Outsider), the wielder revives 1d4 hours later with 1 HP.
   - **Price:** Each resurrection deals 1d4 permanent Wisdom drain due to horrific visions of the afterlife.
3. **Ash-Dipped Dagger**
   - **Power:** On a successful critical hit against an undead, the target is permanently paralyzed in a death-like state.
   - **Price:** If the dagger is removed, the undead awakens. The wielder's maximum HP is reduced by 2 per HD of the paralyzed creature while the dagger remains embedded.
4. **The Celestial Ascendant**
   - **Power:** Can transport the wielder and up to 5 allies to a parallel demiplane (a "prison world") once per month.
   - **Price:** The wielder loses the ability to cast or benefit from Conjuration (Teleportation) magic for 30 days after use.
5. **Grimoire of the First Siphon**
   - **Power:** Wielder can absorb spell effects targeted at them, converting the spell levels into temporary spell slots.
   - **Price:** The wielder loses the ability to naturally regenerate mana/spell slots through resting; they must steal magic to cast.
6. **Vial of the Doppelgänger's Blood**
   - **Power:** When consumed, acts as a *True Resurrection* or completely cures any supernatural affliction/curse.
   - **Price:** The imbiber becomes a beacon for malevolent forces, suffering random encounters with hostile outsiders twice as often.
7. **The Curing Crystal**
   - **Power:** Strips any creature of templates (e.g., Vampire, Lycanthrope), returning them to their base mortal form.
   - **Price:** The user rapidly ages, taking a cumulative -1 penalty to physical stats every week until they die of old age.
8. **Pendant of the Five (Hunter's Mark)**
   - **Power:** Grants +4 favored enemy bonus against Undead and immunity to mind-affecting effects.
   - **Price:** The wielder is compelled to hunt undead; if they go a week without slaying one, they suffer intense hallucinations (fatigued and shaken).
9. **The Phoenix Stone**
   - **Power:** Can trap the soul of a slain enemy, preventing resurrection by any means short of a deity.
   - **Price:** The wielder experiences the trapped souls' worst memories in their nightmares, requiring a DC 20 Will save every night to gain the benefits of rest.
10. **Stake of the Ancient Oak**
   - **Power:** Bypasses all damage reduction of undead creatures and deals an extra 3d6 damage.
   - **Price:** The weapon constantly whispers to vampires and intelligent undead within 1 mile, revealing the wielder's exact location.
11. **The Anchor's Chain**
   - **Power:** Wielder can see and converse with the spirits of the dead globally.
   - **Price:** Every time a creature dies within 100 feet, the wielder takes 1d6 physical backlash damage from the soul passing through them.
12. **Blade of the Traveler**
   - **Power:** Can permanently strip a target of their spellcasting abilities on a critical hit (Will DC 25 negates).
   - **Price:** The wielder can never sleep in the same building/camp twice; doing so causes the building to slowly decay and collapse on them.
13. **Moonstone of Binding**
   - **Power:** Can suppress or force the transformation of any lycanthrope within 60 feet.
   - **Price:** The wielder becomes violently allergic to silver (touching it causes 1d6 acid damage/round) and takes a -4 penalty against lunar magic.
14. **Chalice of the Hybrid**
   - **Power:** Grants the wielder the ability to shift into a dire wolf form while retaining all spellcasting and mental faculties.
   - **Price:** The wielder must consume the blood of an intelligent humanoid once per week or become permanently trapped in wolf form.
15. **Siren's Tuning Fork**
   - **Power:** Emits a frequency that dominates the minds of up to 5 humanoids (as *Dominate Person*, no save).
   - **Price:** The wielder slowly loses their own memories, forgetting one important person or event from their past each time it's used.
16. **The Gemini Dagger**
   - **Power:** Allows two spellcasters to merge their minds, sharing spell slots and casting at +2 caster level.
   - **Price:** If one dies while merged, the other instantly dies as well.
17. **Amulet of the Huntress**
   - **Power:** Grants the wielder an extra life; upon reaching 0 HP, they instantly heal to full and gain *Haste*.
   - **Price:** Every time the extra life is used, an innocent person known to the wielder mysteriously dies in their place.
18. **Staff of the Arcadius**
   - **Power:** Can summon hellfire (unholy fire damage that ignores resistance) as a 60ft cone (10d6 damage).
   - **Price:** The wielder's soul is slowly tethered to the Lower Planes; they can never be resurrected if killed.
19. **The Armory's Bell**
   - **Power:** When rung, instantly deafens and stuns all supernatural creatures within 100 feet for 1d4 rounds.
   - **Price:** The ringer permanently loses 10% of their hearing with each use, eventually going permanently deaf.
20. **Tear of the Sire**
   - **Power:** Any creature that drinks a drop of blood mixed with this tear is charmed by the wielder indefinitely.
   - **Price:** The wielder shares the physical pain of anyone charmed this way.

## True Blood & The Southern Vampire Mysteries
21. **Vial of the Progenitor (Lilith's Blood)**
   - **Power:** Imbiber gains +6 Strength, +6 Dexterity, and Fast Healing 10 for 1 hour.
   - **Price:** Induces severe addiction and bloody rage. Upon wearing off, the user is Exhausted and must make a DC 25 Will save or go on a murderous frenzy.
22. **Pendant of the Fae-Light**
   - **Power:** Shoots a beam of concentrated sunlight (10d6 damage) that blinds targets.
   - **Price:** Drains the wielder's lifeforce. Using the beam deals 2d6 Constitution damage to the wielder.
23. **Chains of the Inquisition (Silvered Bindings)**
   - **Power:** Any shapechanger or undead bound by these chains cannot use supernatural abilities or change form.
   - **Price:** The chains brand the wielder's hands with holy burns, permanently reducing Dexterity by 2 while owned.
24. **Monocle of True Glamour**
   - **Power:** Grants *True Seeing* continuously and immunity to enchantments/charms.
   - **Price:** The wielder perceives the world in horrifying, grotesque decay, causing a permanent -4 penalty to Charisma-based skill checks.
25. **The Maenad's Chalice**
   - **Power:** Any liquid drunk from this chalice casts *Heroes' Feast* and *Rage* on all who partake.
   - **Price:** Drinkers are compelled to participate in violent, chaotic revelry for 24 hours, remembering nothing afterward.
26. **Pelt of the First Shifter**
   - **Power:** Allows the wielder to *Polymorph* into any animal they have physically touched at will.
   - **Price:** The wielder adopts the instincts of the animal; rolling a natural 1 on any check forces them to remain in animal form for 1d4 days.
27. **Stake of the True Death**
   - **Power:** On a successful strike against a vampire, the vampire turns into a puddle of bloody sludge instantly.
   - **Price:** The stake is parasitic; it feeds on the wielder's blood, permanently reducing maximum HP by 5 for every vampire slain.
28. **Collar of the Blood-Bound**
   - **Power:** The wearer shares a telepathic bond with the wielder and must obey their commands.
   - **Price:** If the wearer takes damage, the wielder takes an equal amount of damage.
29. **The Authority's Scepter**
   - **Power:** Can cast *Dictum* or *Blasphemy* once per day at caster level 20.
   - **Price:** The scepter enforces rigid laws; if the wielder ever tells a lie, the scepter strikes them with *Feeblemind*.
30. **Ledger of Fangtasia**
   - **Power:** Contains the true names and secrets of thousands of supernatural entities, granting +10 on Knowledge (Local/Religion).
   - **Price:** Reading it draws the attention of those entities. The wielder has a 10% chance every day of an assassination attempt by a supernatural creature.
31. **Mask of the Ifrit**
   - **Power:** Grants immunity to fire and the ability to control existing flames within 100 feet.
   - **Price:** The wearer’s emotions manifest as localized weather; intense anger causes spontaneous combustion of nearby objects and allies.
32. **The Medium's Séance Cloth**
   - **Power:** Allows the wielder to temporarily channel a dead spirit, gaining its skills and feats for 10 minutes.
   - **Price:** The spirit has a 20% chance to possess the wielder permanently, requiring an exorcism.
33. **Compass of the Blood Tracker**
   - **Power:** Points directly to any creature whose blood has touched the compass, regardless of planar boundaries.
   - **Price:** The compass also broadcasts the wielder's location to the target.
34. **Stone of the Packmaster**
   - **Power:** Grants the wielder control over all canine creatures and lycanthropes within 1 mile.
   - **Price:** The wielder must defeat challengers in unarmed combat; if they refuse a challenge, the stone shatters and inflicts a horrific curse.
35. **The Fae Portal Crystal**
   - **Power:** Opens a gateway to the realm of the Fey.
   - **Price:** For every minute spent in the Fey realm, a year passes in the material plane.
36. **Dagger of Silver Sickness**
   - **Power:** Wounds inflicted by this dagger cannot be healed by magic or fast healing.
   - **Price:** The wielder slowly suffers heavy metal poisoning, taking 1 point of Constitution drain every month they possess it.
37. **Flask of the Synthetic Blood**
   - **Power:** Generates a nutritious fluid that perfectly satisfies the dietary needs of any undead or monster.
   - **Price:** Prolonged use suppresses the creature's natural abilities; vampires lose their fast healing and strength bonuses after 1 month of consumption.
38. **Ashes of the Grand Witch**
   - **Power:** Can be used to cast *Wish* once.
   - **Price:** The witch's spirit is resurrected in the body of the one who made the wish, erasing their original soul.
39. **Idol of the Blood God**
   - **Power:** Grants a vampire wielder the ability to walk in the sun and immunity to stakes.
   - **Price:** The wielder must sacrifice one willing, innocent humanoid on an altar every new moon, or instantly turn to ash.
40. **The Telepath's Crown**
   - **Power:** Wielder can read the surface thoughts of everyone within 60 feet simultaneously.
   - **Price:** The sheer volume of thoughts causes chronic migraines; wielder takes a permanent -4 penalty to Concentration checks and Initiative.

## The Keeper Chronicles (And General Epic Fantasy)
41. **Keystone of the Waygates**
   - **Power:** Allows instant teleportation between ancient, ruined teleportation circles across the world.
   - **Price:** The portals are guarded by dimensional horrors; there is a 10% chance a horrific monster follows the wielder through the gate.
42. **Tome of the Grand Archivist**
   - **Power:** Contains every spell of 4th level or lower. Wielder can cast any of them without preparation once per day.
   - **Price:** The book feeds on memories; the wielder permanently forgets one cherished memory every time a spell is cast from it.
43. **Amulet of the Dragon-Bond**
   - **Power:** Grants the wielder the breath weapon of an ancient dragon (14d6 damage, 1/day).
   - **Price:** The wielder begins to grow scales and hoard gold, eventually becoming an NPC dragon driven only by greed.
44. **Diviner of the Ley-Lines**
   - **Power:** Allows the wielder to tap into planetary magic, doubling the area and duration of all cast spells.
   - **Price:** The planetary magic is volatile; rolling an odd number on a caster level check causes a localized magical explosion (d6 damage per spell level to the caster).
45. **Blade of the First Keeper**
   - **Power:** A +5 Defending Longsword that grants the wielder spell resistance equal to 15 + character level.
   - **Price:** The sword compels the wielder to protect a specific, seemingly random location. Moving more than 1 mile from it inflicts negative levels.
46. **The Memory Sphere**
   - **Power:** Can flawlessly record and replay any event the wielder witnesses.
   - **Price:** The wielder loses the ability to form new long-term memories naturally; they can only remember what they store in the sphere.
47. **Star-Glass of the Elves**
   - **Power:** Illuminates the darkness with starlight, revealing all invisible creatures, illusions, and hidden doors within 120 feet.
   - **Price:** The light attracts creatures from the Shadow Plane. Prolonged use guarantees an encounter with a Nightwing or Shadow Mastiff.
48. **Cloak of the Shadow-Walker**
   - **Power:** Grants *Greater Invisibility* and allows stepping between shadows (as *Dimension Door*).
   - **Price:** The wielder’s shadow takes on a life of its own, occasionally attacking the wielder's allies when their back is turned.
49. **Gauntlet of the Earth-Shaper**
   - **Power:** Wielder can mold stone and earth like clay (as *Move Earth* and *Stone Shape* at will).
   - **Price:** The wielder's flesh slowly petrifies. Every month, they lose 10 feet of movement speed until they become a permanent statue.
50. **Loom of the Time-Weaver**
   - **Power:** Allows the wielder to rewind time by up to 1 minute, re-doing a single combat encounter or conversation.
   - **Price:** The wielder ages 10 years for every minute rewound.
51. **Totem of the Dream-Walker**
   - **Power:** Wielder can enter the dreams of others to extract information or implant suggestions (as *Dream* and *Nightmare*).
   - **Price:** If the wielder is killed in the dream world, they fall into a permanent, irreversible coma in the waking world.
52. **Lantern of the Truth-Seeker**
   - **Power:** Forces anyone in its light to speak only the absolute truth.
   - **Price:** The lantern also reveals the wielder's deepest, darkest secrets to everyone present.
53. **Ring of the Void-Sealer**
   - **Power:** Can permanently close any planar portal or banish an outsider back to its home plane with a touch.
   - **Price:** The wielder is slowly erased from existence; NPCs begin to forget who they are, and eventually, they vanish entirely.
54. **Chains of the Spirit-Binder**
   - **Power:** Can bind a ghost or elemental to an object, granting that object magical properties.
   - **Price:** The bound spirits constantly scream in the wielder's mind, imposing a permanent -2 penalty to all Will saves.
55. **Staff of the Storm-Caller**
   - **Power:** Grants control over the weather in a 5-mile radius (as *Control Weather*).
   - **Price:** The wielder becomes a living lightning rod; during natural storms, they are repeatedly struck by lightning (taking damage each time).
56. **Horn of the Beast-Speaker**
   - **Power:** Summons 1d4 dire animals that follow the wielder's commands for 1 hour.
   - **Price:** The wielder loses the ability to speak humanoid languages while owning the horn, communicating only in grunts and roars.
57. **Amulet of the Stone-Heart**
   - **Power:** Grants immunity to critical hits, sneak attacks, and mind-affecting spells.
   - **Price:** The wielder loses all ability to feel emotion (empathy, love, fear), often alienating allies and making ruthless, sociopathic decisions.
58. **Crystal of Clarity**
   - **Power:** Grants +10 to all Intelligence-based checks and the ability to perfectly predict an opponent's next move (+4 AC).
   - **Price:** The overwhelming influx of data causes physical paralysis; wielder's Dexterity drops to 3 while holding it.
59. **Blade of the Shadow-Bane**
   - **Power:** Deals double damage to evil outsiders and sheds daylight.
   - **Price:** The blade is profoundly racist against anything with dark magic; it forces the wielder to attack tieflings, warlocks, or necromancers on sight.
60. **The Chalice of Equilibrium**
   - **Power:** Can transfer wounds, diseases, and curses from one creature to another.
   - **Price:** If not used once a week, it begins transferring diseases from random commoners into the wielder.

## Harry Potter Extended Universe
61. **Wand of Destiny (The Elder Wand)**
   - **Power:** A +5 spell-storing wand. Any spell cast with it has its DC increased by +4 and bypasses spell resistance.
   - **Price:** A powerful aura of superiority makes the wielder the target of every ambitious mage. If disarmed or defeated, the wand shifts allegiance and the previous wielder loses 2d4 caster levels permanently.
62. **The Resurrection Stone**
   - **Power:** Summons the physical, tangible spirit of a deceased loved one or ally.
   - **Price:** The spirit is hollow and miserable, constantly urging the wielder to commit suicide to join them in the afterlife.
63. **Cloak of True Invisibility**
   - **Power:** Grants absolute invisibility that cannot be pierced by *True Seeing*, *See Invisibility*, or divine senses.
   - **Price:** The cloak masks the wielder from reality itself. While worn, they cannot interact with physical objects, cast spells on others, or be healed.
64. **The Chronometer (Time-Turner)**
   - **Power:** Allows the wielder to travel back in time up to 24 hours to change events.
   - **Price:** The timeline self-corrects violently. If the wielder changes a death, another equally important ally will die in a bizarre accident to balance the scales.
65. **Parchment of the Marauder**
   - **Power:** A map that perfectly tracks the real-time location and true identity of every creature within a 1-mile radius.
   - **Price:** The map actively insults and demoralizes the wielder based on their deepest insecurities, forcing a DC 15 Will save to avoid being Sickened for 1 hour after reading it.
66. **Helm of Assessment (Sorting Hat)**
   - **Power:** When placed on a creature's head, it reveals their exact alignment, class levels, and secret motivations.
   - **Price:** The helm broadcasts this information telepathically to everyone within 100 feet.
67. **The Alchemist's Catalyst (Sorcerer's Stone)**
   - **Power:** Can transmute base metals into solid gold and create an elixir that halts aging.
   - **Price:** The user’s soul becomes brittle. Any critical hit against them has a 10% chance to shatter their soul, causing instant, permanent death.
68. **Basin of the Pensieve**
   - **Power:** Wielder can extract a memory and view it from a third-person, omniscient perspective, noticing details they missed.
   - **Price:** Once extracted, the wielder physically forgets the memory until they drink it back; if the basin is destroyed, the memories are lost forever.
69. **Mirror of Deepest Desires (Erised)**
   - **Power:** Gazing into the mirror grants temporary answers to the wielder's most desperate problems (acting as a *Commune* spell).
   - **Price:** The mirror is deeply addictive. The wielder must make a DC 25 Will save to look away; failure means they starve to death staring at their idealized self.
70. **The Soul-Anchor Amulet (Horcrux)**
   - **Power:** Grants absolute immortality; the wielder cannot die as long as the amulet is intact.
   - **Price:** To create it, the wielder must murder an innocent in cold blood. The wielder's physical body becomes horribly deformed and monstrous, losing Charisma permanently.
71. **Goblet of Binding Contracts**
   - **Power:** Anyone who writes their name and drops it into the goblet is bound by a magical contract to complete a specific task.
   - **Price:** If the task is failed or refused, the signatory loses their magical abilities forever.
72. **Sword of the Griffin (Godric's Sword)**
   - **Power:** A sword that absorbs the properties of anything it slays (e.g., slaying a venomous beast makes the blade permanently venomous).
   - **Price:** The sword becomes heavier with every property absorbed. Eventually, it requires an absurdly high Strength score just to lift.
73. **Fang of the Serpent King (Basilisk)**
   - **Power:** A dagger that deals 2d10 Constitution drain on a hit. It destroys any magical object it pierces.
   - **Price:** The venom is so potent it slowly melts the wielder's hands; they take 1 point of Constitution drain every day they wield it.
74. **The Light-Eater (Deluminator)**
   - **Power:** Can absorb all ambient light in a 100-foot radius, plunging the area into magical darkness.
   - **Price:** The absorbed light must be released; doing so blinds the wielder for 1d4 days.
75. **Sphere of the Remembrall**
   - **Power:** Glows red if the wielder is under a charm, illusion, or memory-altering effect.
   - **Price:** It does not tell the wielder *what* they forgot or are influenced by, causing extreme paranoia and a -2 penalty to all Charisma interactions.
76. **Quill of Blood Punishment**
   - **Power:** Writes on parchment using the blood of the user, creating a magically binding contract that cannot be broken.
   - **Price:** The words written are permanently scarred onto the back of the writer's hand, inflicting 1 point of permanent physical damage per word.
77. **The Sneakoscope**
   - **Power:** Spins and whistles loudly when someone untrustworthy or deceitful is within 30 feet.
   - **Price:** It triggers on *any* deceit, including white lies told by allies, making stealth or diplomacy nearly impossible.
78. **The Foe-Glass**
   - **Power:** A mirror that shows the faces of the wielder's enemies; the closer they are, the clearer the image.
   - **Price:** The mirror induces severe paranoia. The wielder treats all allies as hostile (losing flanking benefits and beneficial spell sharing) if an enemy is close.
79. **Locket of the Serpent-Speaker**
   - **Power:** Grants the ability to speak to reptiles and command them as if using *Dominate Monster*.
   - **Price:** The locket constantly whispers dark intrusive thoughts, forcing the wielder to make a Will save to avoid taking evil actions.
80. **Diadem of Infinite Wisdom**
   - **Power:** Grants a permanent +6 enhancement bonus to Intelligence and Wisdom.
   - **Price:** The sheer weight of cosmic knowledge makes the wielder lethargic; they are permanently staggered (can only take one standard or move action per turn).

## Other Fantasy / Myths / Miscellaneous
81. **The Sovereign Ring (The One Ring - LotR)**
   - **Power:** Grants *Greater Invisibility* and dominance over lesser minds.
   - **Price:** Constantly betrays the wielder to return to its dark master. Prolonged use corrupts alignment to Evil and eventually turns the wielder into a wraith.
82. **Blade of Evil's Bane (Master Sword - Zelda)**
   - **Power:** Automatically dispels evil magic on contact and shoots beams of light when the wielder is at full HP.
   - **Price:** Only the "pure of heart" can wield it; if the wielder commits an evil act, the sword instantly drains them to 0 HP.
83. **Stele of the Seraphs (Mortal Instruments)**
   - **Power:** Allows the wielder to draw angelic runes on their skin, granting immense temporary buffs (+10 to a stat for 1 min).
   - **Price:** Drawing the runes is excruciatingly painful, dealing 2d6 fire damage. Mortals lacking celestial blood go mad if they use it.
84. **The Mortal Cup (Mortal Instruments)**
   - **Power:** Drinking from it grants a mortal the celestial template and access to angelic magic.
   - **Price:** A high mortality rate. Mortals have a 50% chance to instantly die in agony instead of transforming.
85. **The Mortal Sword (Mortal Instruments)**
   - **Power:** Forces absolute, undeniable truth from anyone the blade touches (no save).
   - **Price:** The wielder must also speak the absolute truth while holding it; the sword burns the hands of liars.
86. **Lightning Bolt of the Sky God (Percy Jackson)**
   - **Power:** A javelin that functions as a *Wand of Chain Lightning* with infinite charges.
   - **Price:** The wielder draws the wrath of the sea and the earth; all water elementals and earth creatures are immediately hostile.
87. **Helm of the Underworld (Helm of Darkness)**
   - **Power:** Wielder becomes entirely undetectable (scent, sight, magic, sound) and can pass through solid matter.
   - **Price:** The wielder slowly phases out of reality. If worn for more than 1 hour a day, they become permanently incorporeal.
88. **Trident of the Oceans**
   - **Power:** Grants absolute control over water, creating tsunamis or parting seas.
   - **Price:** The wielder begins to desiccate; they must remain submerged in salt water for 8 hours a day or suffer Constitution drain.
89. **The Golden Fleece**
   - **Power:** Placed on a dying land or person, it rapidly heals and restores them to perfect health and fertility.
   - **Price:** It attracts terrifying monsters (dragons, hydras) who are drawn to its magical resonance to claim it.
90. **The Subtle Knife (His Dark Materials)**
   - **Power:** Can slice through the fabric of reality, creating portals to any plane of existence.
   - **Price:** The knife requires a sacrifice of flesh to bond with a new owner (usually severing two fingers). Every portal left open breeds soul-eating specters.
91. **The Truth Measure (Alethiometer)**
   - **Power:** Answers any question asked of it with absolute truth, interpreting complex symbols.
   - **Price:** It requires an immense Intelligence check to read; failing the check drives the reader temporarily insane for 1d4 days.
92. **The Amber Spyglass**
   - **Power:** Allows the wielder to see the flow of magic and the souls of living creatures.
   - **Price:** The wielder can no longer see physical light; they perceive the world entirely in abstract magical auras.
93. **Chalice of the Spice (Dune)**
   - **Power:** Grants prescience (constant *Foresight* spell) and vastly expands the wielder's lifespan.
   - **Price:** Severe addiction. If the wielder stops drinking it, they suffer massive withdrawal and die within weeks.
94. **Fang of the Sandworm (Crysknife)**
   - **Power:** A blade that bypasses any non-magical armor and cannot be broken.
   - **Price:** If drawn, it must draw blood before being sheathed, even if it is the wielder's own blood. It dissolves if taken off the desert plane.
95. **The Box of Pain (Gom Jabbar)**
   - **Power:** Testing device. If a target keeps their hand inside while being subjected to the illusion of horrific pain, their Will saves permanently increase by +2.
   - **Price:** If the target removes their hand, they are instantly struck with a lethal poison (Fortitude DC 30).
96. **Portal Wardrobe (Narnia)**
   - **Power:** A permanent gateway to a timeless demiplane where the wielder can rest or rule for decades.
   - **Price:** Time does not pass in the real world. The wielder retains the mental age and trauma of their time there, but their physical body resets, creating massive cognitive dissonance.
97. **The Ring of Eternal Winter**
   - **Power:** Grants absolute control over ice and snow, and immunity to cold.
   - **Price:** The wielder's heart literally freezes. They lose all capacity for empathy and their alignment shifts to Neutral Evil.
98. **The Vorpal Sword (Alice in Wonderland)**
   - **Power:** On a critical hit, the sword automatically severs the target's head.
   - **Price:** The sword possesses a chaotic sentience; it frequently demands riddles from the wielder. Failure to answer means the sword refuses to unsheathe.
99. **Tome of the Mad Arab (Necronomicon)**
   - **Power:** Contains rituals to summon ancient, alien deities and cast epic-level necromancy.
   - **Price:** Reading it causes permanent Wisdom drain and summons aberrant horrors to the wielder's location while they sleep.
100. **The Lament Configuration (Hellraiser)**
   - **Power:** A puzzle box that, when solved, grants the wielder ultimate physical pleasure and sensory perfection.
   - **Price:** The "pleasure" is delivered by extradimensional sadomasochistic outsiders (Cenobites) who drag the wielder to a realm of eternal torture.
