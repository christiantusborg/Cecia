# Major Artifact: The Necklace of the Seven

**Description:**  
A deceptively simple-looking silver chain that seems to hum with a low, vibrating frequency. It features seven empty sockets, each designed to hold a specific soul-gem or "Echo-Stone." When all seven stones are gathered, the necklace grants the wielder near-godlike control over the memories and identities of the world, but at the cost of their own singular existence.

## The Law of Equilibrium

### The Power: The Unified Echo
The necklace acts as a focusing lens for the **Gilded Harvesters'** collective power. 

### The Burden: The Cacophony of Souls
Every stone added to the necklace increases the number of voices whispering in the wielder's mind. For every stone socketed, the wielder takes a cumulative -2 penalty to Will saves against possession or insanity. If all seven are socketed, the wielder must make a DC 35 Will save every hour or be permanently absorbed into the collective consciousness of the stones.
