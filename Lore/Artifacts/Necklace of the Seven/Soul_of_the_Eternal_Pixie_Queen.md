# Great Artifact: The Soul of the Eternal Pixie Queen (Stone I)

**Description:**  
A tear-shaped emerald that glows with a shifting, internal light. It pulses in sync with the heartbeat of anyone who holds it. This was the first artifact created by the Queen, containing the core of her own essence and memories before her physical body was ever imprisoned.

## The Law of Equilibrium

### The Power: Queenly Possession
Any **Pixie** who wears the *Necklace of the Seven* while this stone is socketed is immediately subjected to a possession attempt.

*   **Mechanic (3.5 rules):** The wearer must succeed on a **DC 30 Will Save**. 
*   **Success:** The wearer retains control but can hear the Queen's whispers, providing a +4 Insight bonus to Knowledge (History) and Knowledge (Arcana).
*   **Failure:** The wearer's soul is suppressed, and the **Eternal Pixie Queen** takes full control of the body. She retains her own Intelligence (24), Wisdom (22), and Charisma (26), and her full caster level (estimated at 20+), while using the physical stats of the host.

### The Burden: The Anchor of Fate
Even if the save is successful, the stone acts as an anchor. The wielder cannot remove the necklace through any means short of a *Mage's Disjunction* or the intervention of a deity. Furthermore, the wielder becomes a "Beacon" for other Gilded Harvesters, who can sense the Queen's essence from miles away.

---

## Lore and History
The Queen created this stone as a safeguard against her own mortality or imprisonment. By splitting her soul, she ensured that even if her physical form was captured by heroes like **Anlyth of Daefaren**, her mind could return through any Pixie foolish or ambitious enough to wear her chain. 

It is rumored that during her imprisonment in **9897 EE**, she attempted to lure one of Anlyth's subordinates into wearing the stone, an attempt that was only thwarted by Anlyth's own deep knowledge of soul-magic.
